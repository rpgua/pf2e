 **Source:** Player Core pg. 287


 This satchel includes 50 feet of [rope](/Equipment.aspx?ID=44), pulleys, a dozen [pitons](/Equipment.aspx?ID=39), a hammer, a [grappling hook](/Equipment.aspx?ID=23), and one set of crampons. Climbing kits allow you to attach yourself to the wall you're [Climbing](/Actions.aspx?ID=33), moving half as quickly as usual (minimum 5 feet) but letting you attempt a DC 5 flat check whenever you critically fail to prevent a fall. A single kit has only enough materials for one climber; each climber needs their own kit. If you wear your climbing kit, you can access it as part of a Climb action.
