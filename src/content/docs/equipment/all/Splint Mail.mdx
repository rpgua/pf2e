 This type of armor is chain mail reinforced with flexible, interlocking metal plates, typically located on the wearer's torso, upper arms, and legs. A suit of this armor comes with an undercoat of [padded armor](/Armor.aspx?ID=3) (see above) and a pair of [gauntlets](/Weapons.aspx?ID=4).
