 Half plate consists of most of the upper body plates used in [full plate](/Armor.aspx?ID=13), with lighter or sparser steel plate protection for the arms and legs. This provides some of the protection of full plate with greater flexibility and speed. A suit of this armor comes with an undercoat of [padded armor](/Armor.aspx?ID=3) and a pair of [gauntlets](/Weapons.aspx?ID=4).
