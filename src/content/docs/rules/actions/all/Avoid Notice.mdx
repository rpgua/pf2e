<Action title={"Avoid Notice"} traits={[['Exploration']]} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2622"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 438

  You attempt a [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) check to avoid notice while traveling at half speed. If you're Avoiding Notice at the start of an encounter, you usually roll a Stealth check instead of a Perception check both to determine your initiative and to see if the enemies notice you (based on their Perception DCs, as normal for Sneak, regardless of their initiative check results).
