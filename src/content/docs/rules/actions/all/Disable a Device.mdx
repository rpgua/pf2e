 **Source:** Player Core pg. 247

  This action allows you to disarm a trap or another complex device. Often, a device requires numerous successes before becoming disabled, depending on its construction and complexity. A [thieves' toolkit](/Equipment.aspx?ID=2758) is helpful and sometimes even required to Disable a Device, as determined by the GM, and sometimes a device requires a higher proficiency rank in [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17) to disable it.

 Your Thievery check result determines your progress.

**Critical Success** You disable the device, or you achieve two successes toward disabling a device requiring more than one success. You leave no trace of your tampering, and you can rearm the device later, if that type of device can be rearmed.<br />
**Success** You disable the device, or you achieve one success toward disabling a device that requires more than one success.<br />
**Critical Failure** You trigger the device.
