<Action title={"Master Strike"} traits={[['Incapacitation'], ['Rogue']]} action={0} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2259"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 169

  The target attempts a Fortitude save against your class DC. It then becomes temporarily immune to your Master Strike for 1 day.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is [enfeebled 2](/Conditions.aspx?ID=13) until the end of your next turn.<br />
**Failure** The target is [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28) for 4 rounds.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is paralyzed for 4 rounds, knocked [unconscious](/Conditions.aspx?ID=38) for 2 hours, or killed (your choice).

 </Action>
