<Action title={"Identify Alchemy"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Exploration'], ['Secret']]} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2386"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 237

  You can identify the nature of an [alchemical](/Traits.aspx?ID=6) item with 10 minutes of testing using your alchemist's toolkit. If your attempt is interrupted in any way, you must start over. 

**Success** You identify the item and the means of activating it.<br />
**Failure** You fail to identify the item but can try again.<br />
**Critical Failure** You misidentify the item as another item of the GM's choice.
