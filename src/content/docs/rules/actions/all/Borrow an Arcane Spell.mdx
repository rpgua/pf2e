<Action title={"Borrow an Arcane Spell"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Exploration']]} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2373"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 234

  If you're an arcane spellcaster who prepares spells, you can attempt to prepare a spell from someone else's arcane spellbook, arcane witch familiar, or the like. The GM sets the DC for the check based on the spell's rank and rarity; it's typically a bit easier than [Learning the Spell](/Actions.aspx?ID=25).

**Success** You prepare the borrowed spell as part of your normal spell preparation.<br />
**Failure** You fail to prepare the spell, but the spell slot remains available for you to prepare a different spell. You can't try to borrow this spell again until the next time you prepare spells.

 </Action>
