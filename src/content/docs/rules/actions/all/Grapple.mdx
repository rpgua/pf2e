<Action title={"Grapple"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2376"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 235

  Attempt an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check against the target's Fortitude DC. You can Grapple a target you already have [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20) or [restrained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=33) without having a hand free.

**Critical Success** Your target is restrained until the end of your next turn unless you move or your target [Escapes](/Actions.aspx?ID=79).<br />
**Success** Your target is grabbed until the end of your next turn unless you move or your target Escapes.<br />
**Failure** You fail to grab your target. If you already had the target grabbed or restrained using a Grapple, those conditions on the target end.<br />
**Critical Failure** If you already had the target grabbed or restrained, it breaks free. Your target can either grab you, as if it succeeded at using the Grapple action against you, or force you to fall and land [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31).

 </Action>
