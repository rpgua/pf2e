 **Source:** Player Core pg. 138

  You lash out at a foe that leaves an opening. Make a melee Strike against the triggering creature. If your attack is a critical hit and the trigger was a [manipulate](/Traits.aspx?ID=104) action, you [disrupt](/Rules.aspx?ID=394) that action. This Strike doesn't count toward your multiple attack penalty, and your multiple attack penalty doesn't apply to this Strike.
