<Action title={"Trip"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2382"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 236

  You try to knock a creature to the ground. Attempt an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check against the target's Reflex DC.

**Critical Success** The target falls, lands [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31), and takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage.<br />
**Success** The target falls and lands prone.<br />
**Critical Failure** You lose your balance, fall, and land prone.
