<Action title={"Balance"} traits={[['Move']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2369"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 233

  You move across a narrow surface or uneven ground, attempting an [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) check against its Balance DC. You are [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) while on a narrow surface or uneven ground.

**Critical Success** You move up to your Speed.<br />
**Success** You move up to your Speed, treating it as [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453) (every 5 feet costs 10 feet of movement).<br />
**Failure** You must remain stationary to keep your balance (wasting the action) or you fall. If you fall, your turn ends.<br />
**Critical Failure** You fall and your turn ends.

 </Action>
