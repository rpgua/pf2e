<Action title={"Reposition"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2379"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 235

  You muscle a creature or object around. Attempt an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check against the target's Fortitude DC.

**Critical Success** You [move](/Rules.aspx?ID=451) the creature up to 10 feet. It must remain within your reach during this movement, and you can't move it into or through obstacles.<br />
**Success** You move the target up to 5 feet. It must remain within your reach during this movement, and you can't move it into or through obstacles.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target can move you up to 5 feet as though it successfully Repositioned you.

 </Action>
