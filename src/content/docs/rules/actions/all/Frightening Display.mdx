 **Source:** Player Core pg. 206

  Your ape makes a loud and frightening display, throwing a foe off balance. The ape attempts to [Demoralize](/Actions.aspx?ID=53) the target creature; this display gains the [visual](/Traits.aspx?ID=163) trait and doesn't require language. While frightened by this ability, the target is [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) to your ape.
