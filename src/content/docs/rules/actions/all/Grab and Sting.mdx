<Action title={"Grab and Sting"} traits={[]} action={4} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2283"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 210

  The scorpion holds its foe in place with its pincers in order to sting it. It makes a pincer Strike. If the Strike hits, the target creature automatically becomes [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20) by the scorpion, which then makes a stinger Strike against it. The grabbed condition lasts until the end of your next turn.
