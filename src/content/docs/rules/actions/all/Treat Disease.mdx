<Action title={"Treat Disease"} traits={[['Downtime'], ['Manipulate']]} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2397"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 242

  You spend at least 8 hours caring for a diseased creature. Attempt a [Medicine](/Skills.aspx?ID=9) check against the [disease's](/Traits.aspx?ID=578) DC. After you attempt to Treat a Disease for a creature, you can't try again until after that creature's next save against the disease.

**Critical Success** You grant the creature a +4 circumstance bonus to its next saving throw against the disease.<br />
**Success** You grant the creature a +2 circumstance bonus to its next saving throw against the disease.<br />
**Critical Failure** Your efforts cause the creature to take a –2 circumstance penalty to its next save against the disease.

 </Action>
