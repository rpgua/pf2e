<Action title={"Take Cover"} traits={[]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2307"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 418

  You press yourself against a wall or duck behind an obstacle to take better advantage of cover. If you would have standard cover, you instead gain greater cover, which provides a +4 circumstance bonus to AC; to Reflex saves against area effects; and to [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) checks to [Hide](/Actions.aspx?ID=62), [Sneak](/Actions.aspx?ID=63), or otherwise avoid detection. Otherwise, you gain the benefits of standard cover (a +2 circumstance bonus instead). This lasts until you move from your current space, use an [attack](/Traits.aspx?ID=15) action, become [unconscious](/Conditions.aspx?ID=38), or end this effect as a free action.
