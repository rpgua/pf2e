<Action title={"Affix a Talisman"} traits={[['Exploration'], ['Manipulate']]} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2632"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 439

  You spend 10 minutes affixing a [talisman](/Traits.aspx?ID=155) to an item, placing the item on a stable surface and using the repair toolkit with both hands. You can also use this activity to remove a talisman. Attaching more than one talisman to an item deactivates all the talismans. They must be removed and re-affixed before they can be used again.

 </Action>
