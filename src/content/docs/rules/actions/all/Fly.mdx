<Action title={"Fly"} traits={[['Move']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2312"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 419

  You move through the air up to your fly Speed. Moving upward (straight up or diagonally) uses the rules for moving through [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453). You can move straight down 10 feet for every 5 feet of movement you spend. If you Fly to the ground, you don't take [falling damage](/Rules.aspx?ID=402). You can use an action to Fly 0 feet to hover in place. If you're airborne at the end of your turn and didn't use a Fly action this round, you fall.
