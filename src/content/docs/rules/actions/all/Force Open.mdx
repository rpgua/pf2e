<Action title={"Force Open"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2375"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 234

  Using your body, a lever, or some other tool, you attempt to forcefully open a door, window, container or heavy gate. With a high enough result, you can even smash through walls. Without a [crowbar](/Equipment.aspx?ID=18), prying something open takes a –2 item penalty to the [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check to Force Open.

**Critical Success** You open the door, window, container, or gate and can avoid damaging it in the process.<br />
**Success** You break the door, window, container, or gate open, and it gains the [broken](/Conditions.aspx?ID=2) condition. If it's especially sturdy, the GM might have it take damage but not be broken.<br />
**Critical Failure** Your attempt jams the door, window, container, or gate shut, imposing a –2 circumstance penalty on future attempts to Force it Open.
