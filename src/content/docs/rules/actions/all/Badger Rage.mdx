<Action title={"Badger Rage"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Emotion'], ['Mental']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2273"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 207

  The badger enters a state of pure rage that lasts for 1 minute, until there are no enemies it can perceive, or until it falls [unconscious](/Conditions.aspx?ID=38), whichever comes first. It can't voluntarily stop raging. While raging, the badger is affected in the following ways. 
<ul><li>It deals 4 additional damage with its bite attacks and 2 additional damage with its claw attacks.</li><li>It takes a –1 penalty to AC.</li><li>It can't use actions that have the [concentrate](/Traits.aspx?ID=32) trait. The animal companion can [Seek](/Actions.aspx?ID=84) even while raging.</li></ul>After it has stopped raging, it can't use Badger Rage again for 1 minute.

 </Action>
