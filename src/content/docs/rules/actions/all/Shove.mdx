<Action title={"Shove"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2380"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 235

  You push a creature away from you. Attempt an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check against your target's Fortitude DC.

**Critical Success** You [push](/Rules.aspx?ID=451) your target up to 10 feet away from you. You can Stride after it, but you must move the same distance and in the same direction.<br />
**Success** You push your target back 5 feet. You can Stride after it, but you must move the same distance and in the same direction.<br />
**Critical Failure** You lose your balance, fall, and land [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31).

 </Action>
