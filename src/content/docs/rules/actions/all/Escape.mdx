<Action title={"Escape"} traits={[['Attack']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2296"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 416

  You attempt to escape from being [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20), [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24), or [restrained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=33). Choose one creature, object, spell effect, hazard, or other impediment imposing any of those conditions on you. Attempt a check using your unarmed attack modifier against the DC of the effect. This is typically the [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) DC of a creature grabbing you, the [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17) DC of a creature who tied you up, the spell DC for a spell effect, or the listed Escape DC of an object, hazard, or other impediment. You can attempt an [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) or [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check instead of using your attack modifier if you choose (but this action still has the [attack](/Traits.aspx?ID=15) trait).

**Critical Success** You get free and remove the grabbed, immobilized, and restrained conditions imposed by your chosen target. You can then Stride up to 5 feet.<br />
**Success** You get free and remove the grabbed, immobilized, and restrained conditions imposed by your chosen target.<br />
**Critical Failure** You don't get free, and you can't attempt to Escape again until your next turn.

 </Action>
