<Action title={"Tumble Through"} traits={[['Move']]} action={2} level={0} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Actions.aspx?ID=2370"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 233

  You Stride up to your Speed. During this movement, you can try to move through the space of one enemy. Attempt an [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) check against the enemy's Reflex DC as soon as you try to enter its space. You can Tumble Through using [Climb](/Actions.aspx?ID=33), [Fly](/Actions.aspx?ID=94), [Swim](/Actions.aspx?ID=39), or another action instead of Stride in the appropriate environment.

**Success** You move through the enemy's space, treating the squares in its space as difficult terrain (every 5 feet costs 10 feet of movement). If you don't have enough Speed to move all the way through its space, you get the same effect as a failure.<br />
**Failure** Your movement ends, and you trigger reactions as if you had moved out of the square you started in.
