<Condition title={"Blinded"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=59"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 442

  You can't see. All normal terrain is [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453) to you. You can't detect anything using vision. You automatically critically fail Perception checks that require you to be able to see, and if vision is your only [precise sense](/Rules.aspx?ID=411), you take a –4 status penalty to Perception checks. You are immune to [visual](/Traits.aspx?ID=163) effects. Blinded overrides [dazzled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=7).

 </Condition>
