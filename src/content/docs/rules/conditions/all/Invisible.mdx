<Condition title={"Invisible"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=83"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 444

  You can't be seen. You're [undetected](/Conditions.aspx?ID=39) to everyone. Creatures can [Seek](/Actions.aspx?ID=84) to detect you; if a creature succeeds at its Perception check against your [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) DC, you become [hidden](/Conditions.aspx?ID=22) to that creature until you [Sneak](/Actions.aspx?ID=63) to become undetected again. If you become invisible while someone can already see you, you start out hidden to them (instead of undetected) until you successfully Sneak. You can't become [observed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=27) while invisible except via special abilities or magic.

 </Condition>
