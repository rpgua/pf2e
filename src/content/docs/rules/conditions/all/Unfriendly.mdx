<Condition title={"Unfriendly"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=97"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 447

  This condition reflects a creature's disposition toward a particular character, and only supernatural effects (like a spell) can impose this condition on a PC. A creature that is unfriendly to a character dislikes and distrusts that character. The unfriendly creature won't accept [Requests](/Actions.aspx?ID=51) from the character.

 </Condition>
