<Condition title={"Restrained"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=90"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 446

  You're tied up and can barely move, or a creature has you pinned. You have the [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) and [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24) conditions, and you can't use any [attack](/Traits.aspx?ID=15) or [manipulate](/Traits.aspx?ID=104) actions except to attempt to [Escape](/Actions.aspx?ID=79) or [Force Open](/Actions.aspx?ID=34) your bonds. Restrained overrides [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20).

 </Condition>
