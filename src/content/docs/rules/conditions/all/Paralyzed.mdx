<Condition title={"Paralyzed"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=85"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 445

  You're frozen in place. You have the [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) condition and can't act except to [Recall Knowledge](/Actions.aspx?ID=26) and use actions that require only your mind (as determined by the GM). Your senses still function, but only in the areas you can perceive without moving, so you can't [Seek](/Actions.aspx?ID=84).

 </Condition>
