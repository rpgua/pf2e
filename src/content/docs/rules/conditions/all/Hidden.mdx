<Condition title={"Hidden"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=79"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 444

  While you're hidden from a creature, that creature knows the space you're in but can't tell precisely where you are. You typically become hidden by using [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) to [Hide](/Actions.aspx?ID=62). When [Seeking](/Actions.aspx?ID=84) a creature using only [imprecise](/Rules.aspx?ID=412) senses, it remains hidden, rather than [observed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=27). A creature you're hidden from is [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) to you, and it must succeed at a DC 11 flat check when targeting you with an attack, spell, or other effect or it fails to affect you. Area effects aren't subject to this flat check.

 A creature might be able to use the Seek action to try to observe you.
