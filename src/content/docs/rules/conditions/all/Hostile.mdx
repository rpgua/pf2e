<Condition title={"Hostile"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=80"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 444

  This condition reflects a creature's disposition toward a particular character, and only supernatural effects (like a spell) can impose on a PC. A creature hostile to a character actively seeks to harm that character. It doesn't necessarily attack, but it won't accept [Requests](/Actions.aspx?ID=51) from the character.

 </Condition>
