<Condition title={"Immobilized"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=81"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 444

  You are incapable of movement. You can't use any actions that have the [move](/Traits.aspx?ID=114) trait. If you're immobilized by something holding you in place and an external force would move you out of your space, the force must succeed at a check against either the DC of the effect holding you in place or the relevant defense (usually Fortitude DC) of the monster holding you in place.

 </Condition>
