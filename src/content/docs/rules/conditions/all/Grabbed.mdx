<Condition title={"Grabbed"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=77"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 444

  You're held in place by another creature, giving you the [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) and [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24) conditions. If you attempt a [manipulate](/Traits.aspx?ID=104) action while grabbed, you must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or it is lost; roll the check after spending the action, but before any effects are applied.
