<Condition title={"Prone"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=88"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 445

  You're lying on the ground. You are [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) and take a –2 circumstance penalty to attack rolls. The only [move](/Traits.aspx?ID=114) actions you can use while you're prone are [Crawl](/Actions.aspx?ID=76) and [Stand](/Actions.aspx?ID=86). Standing up ends the prone condition. You can [Take Cover](/Actions.aspx?ID=90) while prone to hunker down and gain [greater cover](/Rules.aspx?ID=459) against ranged attacks, even if you don't have an object to get behind, which grants you a +4 circumstance bonus to AC against ranged attacks (but you remain off-guard).

 If you would be knocked prone while you're [Climbing](/Actions.aspx?ID=33) or [Flying](/Actions.aspx?ID=94), you [fall](/Rules.aspx?ID=2352). You can't be knocked prone when [Swimming](/Actions.aspx?ID=39).

 </Condition>
