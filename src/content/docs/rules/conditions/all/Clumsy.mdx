<Condition title={"Clumsy"} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Conditions.aspx?ID=61"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 442

  Your movements become clumsy and inexact. Clumsy always includes a value. You take a status penalty equal to the condition value to Dexterity-based checks and DCs, including AC, Reflex saves, ranged attack rolls, and skill checks using [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1), [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15), and [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17).
