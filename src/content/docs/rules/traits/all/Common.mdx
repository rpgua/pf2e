<Trait title={"Common"} traits={[['Common']]} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=557"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 11

  Anything that doesn't list another rarity trait ([uncommon](/Traits.aspx?ID=159), [rare](/Traits.aspx?ID=137), or [unique](/Traits.aspx?ID=161)) automatically has the common trait. This rarity indicates that an ability, item, or spell is available to all players who meet the prerequisites for it. A creature of this rarity is generally known and can be summoned with the appropriate _summon_ spell.

 </Trait>
