You create a pattern of lights that pulses with intensity. Creatures are [dazzled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=7) while inside the pattern. In addition, a creature must attempt a Will saving throw if it's inside the pattern when you cast it, enters the pattern, ends its turn within the pattern, or uses a Seek or Interact action on the pattern. A creature currently [blinded](/Conditions.aspx?ID=1) by the pattern doesn't need to attempt new saving throws.

**Success** The creature is unaffected.<br />
**Failure** The creature is blinded by the pattern. If it exits the pattern, it can attempt a new save to recover from the blinded condition at the end of each of its turns, to a maximum duration of 1 minute.<br />
**Critical Failure** The creature is blinded for 1 minute.

</Spell>
