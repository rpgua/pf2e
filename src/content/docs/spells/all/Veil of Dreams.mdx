<Spell title={"Veil of Dreams"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Focus'], ['Hex'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental'], ['Witch']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1886"} duration={60} lesson={['Lesson of Dreams']} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 386']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 creature"} type={"Spell"}>
Your patron draws the target into a drowsy state, causing daydreams and sluggishness. The target must attempt a Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target takes a –1 status penalty to Perception, attack rolls, and Will saves. This penalty increases to –2 for Will saves against [sleep](/Traits.aspx?ID=145) effects.<br />
**Failure** As success, and any time the target uses a [concentrate](/Traits.aspx?ID=32) action, it must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or the action is disrupted.

</Spell>
