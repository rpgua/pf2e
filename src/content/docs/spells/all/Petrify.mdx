<Spell title={"Petrify"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Earth'], ['Manipulate']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1628"} element={['Earth']} legacy_name={"Flesh to Stone"} range={120} saving_throw={"Fortitude"} source={['Player Core pg. 349']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature made of organic material"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
The target's body slowly turns into a stone statue. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is [slowed 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35) for 1 round as stone begins to form on their body.<br />
**Failure** The target is slowed 1 and must attempt a Fortitude save at the end of each of its turns; this ongoing save has the [incapacitation](/Traits.aspx?ID=93) trait. On a failed save, the slowed condition increases by 1 (or 2 on a critical failure) as stone growths creep across their body. A successful save reduces the slowed condition by 1. When a creature becomes fully unable to act due to the slowed condition from petrify, the spell then ends in a flash of gray light, leaving the target [petrified](/Conditions.aspx?ID=30) permanently as they become a statue. The spell also ends if the slowed condition is removed, which causes the stone to break off harmlessly.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, but the target is initially slowed 2.

</Spell>
