With soothing words, you lull the target into an enchanting dream. When you Cast the Spell, the target falls [unconscious](/Conditions.aspx?ID=38) if it wasn't already. While unconscious, it experiences a dream of your choice, though lucidly enough it can wake when it pleases. If it wakes up before 1 minute of sleep has passed, the spell ends. 
<ul><li>**Dream of Insight** +1 status bonus to Intelligence-based skill checks</li><li>**Dream of Glamor** +1 status bonus to Charisma-based skill checks</li><li>**Dream of Voyaging** +5-foot status bonus to Speed.</li></ul>  If you Cast this Spell again, any previous sweet dream you cast ends.

---
**Heightened (4th)** The bonus for a dream of insight or glamor is +2.

**Heightened (7th)** The bonus for a dream of insight or glamor is +3.

</Spell>
