You materialize a handheld [musical instrument](/Equipment.aspx?ID=37) in your grasp. The instrument is typical for its type, but it plays for only you. It vanishes when the spell ends. If you cast _summon instrument_ again, any instrument you previously summoned disappears.

---
**Heightened (5th)** The instrument is instead a virtuoso handheld instrument.
