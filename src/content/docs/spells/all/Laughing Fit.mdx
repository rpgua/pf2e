The target is overtaken with uncontrollable laughter. It must attempt a Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is plagued with uncontrollable laugher. It can't use reactions.<br />
**Failure** The target is [slowed 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35) and can't use reactions.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target falls [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31) and can't use actions or reactions for 1 round. It then takes the effects of a failure.
