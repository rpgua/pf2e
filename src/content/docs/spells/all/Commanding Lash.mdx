With the threat of more pain, you compel a creature you've recently harmed. You issue a command to the target, with the effects of the spell command. If the target is [frightened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=19), [stupefied](/Conditions.aspx?ID=37), or taking [persistent damage](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29), it takes a –2 circumstance penalty to the save. Regardless of the result, the target is then temporarily immune for 1 hour.
