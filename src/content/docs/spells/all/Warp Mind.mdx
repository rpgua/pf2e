You scramble a creature's mental faculties and sensory input. The target must attempt a Will saving throw. Regardless of the  result of that save, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes. _Warp mind's_ effects happen instantly, so [_dispel magic_](/Spells.aspx?ID=78) and other effects that [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) spells can't counteract them. However, _wish_ rituals and abilities that can remove non-magical effects can still counteract the effects.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target spends the first action on its next turn with the [confused](/Conditions.aspx?ID=5) condition.<br />
**Failure** The target is confused for 1 minute.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is confused permanently.

</Spell>
