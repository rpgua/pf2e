<Spell title={"Crisis of Faith"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={3} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1478"} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 322']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Divine']} type={"Spell"}>
You assault the target's faith, riddling the creature with doubt and mental turmoil that deal 6d6 mental damage, or 6d8 mental damage if it can cast [divine](/Traits.aspx?ID=48) spells. The effects are determined by its Will save. <br />

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target takes half damage.<br />
**Failure** The target takes full damage; if the target can cast divine spells, it's [stupefied 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=37) for 1 round.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target takes double damage, is stupefied 1 for 1 round, and can't cast divine spells for 1 round.

 To many deities, casting this spell on a follower of your own deity without significant cause is anathema.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d6 (or by 2d8 if the target is a divine spellcaster).

</Spell>
