<Spell title={"Plant Growth"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Plant'], ['Vitality'], ['Wood']]} action={86400} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Rituals.aspx?ID=121"} area={"1/2-mile-radius circle centered on you"} duration={31536000} element={['Wood']} source={['Player Core pg. 394']} type={"Ritual"} primary_check={" Nature  (expert)"} secondary_casters={"1"} secondary_check={"Farming  Lore  or  Survival "}>
You cause the plants within the area to be healthier and more fruitful. In addition to other benefits of healthy plants, this increases the crop yield for farms, depending on your success. If you cast it in the area of a [blight](/Rituals.aspx?ID=4), _plant growth_ attempts to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) the _blight_ instead of producing its usual effect.

**Critical Success** Double the crop yield in the area, or increase the area to a 1-mile radius.<br />
**Success** Increase the crop yield in the area by one-third.<br />
**Failure** The ritual has no effect.<br />
**Critical Failure** The flora in the area changes in an unanticipated way, determined by the GM but generally as contradictory to your true desires as possible (for instance, blighting crops when you would prefer to enrich them).

</Spell>
