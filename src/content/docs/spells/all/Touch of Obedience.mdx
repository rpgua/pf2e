<Spell title={"Touch of Obedience"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Focus'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1844"} domain={['Tyranny']} range={0} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 380']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 living creature"} type={"Spell"}>
Your imperious touch erodes the target's willpower, making it easier to control. The target attempts a Will save. Regardless of the result, it's then temporarily immune for 1 hour.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is stupefied 1 until the end of your next turn.<br />
**Failure** The target is stupefied 2 until the end of your next turn.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is stupefied 2 for 1 minute and is forced to its knees, becoming [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31).

</Spell>
