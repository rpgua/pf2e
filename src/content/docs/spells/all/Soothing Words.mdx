You attempt to calm the target by uttering soothing words in a calm and even tone. The target gains a +1 status bonus to Will saving throws. This bonus increases to +2 against [emotion](/Traits.aspx?ID=60) effects.

 In addition, when you Cast this Spell, you can attempt to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) one emotion effect on the target.

---
**Heightened (5th)** The bonus to saves increases to +2, or +3 against emotion effects.
