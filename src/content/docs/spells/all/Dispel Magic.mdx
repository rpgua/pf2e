You unravel the magic behind a spell or effect. Attempt a [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) check against the target. If you successfully counteract a magic item, it becomes a mundane item of its type for 10 minutes. This doesn't change the item's non-magical properties. If the target is an [artifact](/Traits.aspx?ID=253) or similar item, you automatically fail.
