<Spell title={"Dreamer's Call"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Concentrate'], ['Focus'], ['Illusion'], ['Incapacitation'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1797"} domain={['Dreams']} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} school={"illusion"} source={['Player Core pg. 374']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 creature"} type={"Spell"}>
You create a vivid, illusory daydream drawn from the images of the target's dreams. The daydream appears in an unoccupied space in range, and you attempt to draw the target toward it.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target's attention wavers. It becomes [fascinated](/Conditions.aspx?ID=14) by the daydream.<br />
**Failure** The target is fascinated with the daydream and pursues a course of action you choose as its first action after you Cast the Spell: approach the daydream, run away from it (as the fleeing condition), Release what it's holding as an offering, or [Drop Prone](/Actions.aspx?ID=78) in obeisance.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, but the target follows the course of action for as many actions as possible for the spell's duration, and does nothing else.

</Spell>
