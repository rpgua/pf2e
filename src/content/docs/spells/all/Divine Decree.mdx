<Spell title={"Divine Decree"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Sanctified'], ['Spirit']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={7} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1495"} area={"40-foot emanation"} range={40} saving_throw={"Fortitude"} source={['Player Core pg. 325']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Divine']} type={"Spell"}>
You utter a potent litany from your faith, a mandate that harms those who oppose your ideals. You deal 7d10 spirit damage to your enemies in the area; each enemy must attempt a Fortitude save.

**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The creature takes half damage.<br />
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and is enfeebled 2 for 1 minute.<br />
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and is enfeebled 2 for 1 minute. If you're on your home plane and the creature is not, the creature is sent back to its home plane. A creature of 10th level or lower must also succeed at a Will save or be [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28) for 1 minute; if it critically fails, it dies (this is a [death](/Traits.aspx?ID=40) effect).

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d10, and the level of creatures that must attempt a second save on a critical failure increases by 2.

</Spell>
