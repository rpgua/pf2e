You shroud a creature from others' senses. The target becomes [undetected](/Conditions.aspx?ID=39), not just to sight but to all senses, allowing the target to count as [invisible](/Conditions.aspx?ID=26) no matter what precise and imprecise senses an observer might have. It's still possible for a creature to find the target by [Seeking](/Actions.aspx?ID=84), looking for disturbed dust, hearing gaps in the sound spectrum, or finding some other way to discover the presence of an otherwise-undetectable creature.
