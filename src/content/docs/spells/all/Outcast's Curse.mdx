<Spell title={"Outcast's Curse"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Curse'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental'], ['Misfortune']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1620"} range={0} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 347']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Divine', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
You afflict the target with a curse that makes its presence abrasive and off-putting. The target must attempt a Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** For 10 minutes, the target must roll twice and use the worse result whenever attempting a [Deception](/Skills.aspx?ID=5), [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6), [Intimidation](/Skills.aspx?ID=7), or [Performance](/Skills.aspx?ID=12) check, and creatures they encounter have an initial attitude toward them of one step worse (for instance, [unfriendly](/Conditions.aspx?ID=40) instead of [indifferent](/Conditions.aspx?ID=25)).<br />
**Failure** As success, but the effect is permanent.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, and creatures that the target encounters have an initial attitude toward them of two steps worse.

</Spell>
