You snatch creatures from the jaws of death, restoring them without the strain of a typical close call. You can spend 1 to 3 actions Casting this Spell, and you can target a number of creatures equal to the actions spent. Each target regains 3d6 Hit Points. If the target had the [dying](/Conditions.aspx?ID=11) condition, coming back from dying due to this healing doesn't increase its [wounded](/Conditions.aspx?ID=42) condition.

---
**Heightened (+1)** Increase the healing by 1d6.
