<Spell title={"Mariner's Curse"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Curse'], ['Manipulate']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1593"} range={0} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 342']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
You afflict the target with the curse of the roiling, unforgiving sea. The target must attempt a Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target becomes [sickened 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=34). Reducing its sickened condition to 0 ends the curse.<br />
**Failure** The target becomes sickened 1 and can't reduce its sickened condition below 1 while the curse remains. The curse can be lifted by 4th-rank [_cleanse affliction_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1467) or similar magic. Whenever the target is sickened and on the water at least a mile from shore, it is also [slowed 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35).<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, but the target becomes sickened 2.

</Spell>
