You create a cloud of mesmerizing patterns and colors that hovers in the air. Creatures are [dazzled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=7) while inside the cloud. In addition, a creature must attempt a Will saving throw if it is inside the cloud when you cast it, when it enters the cloud, when it ends its turn within the cloud, or if it uses a [Seek](/Actions.aspx?ID=84) or Interact action on the cloud. A creature currently [fascinated](/Conditions.aspx?ID=14) by _hypnotize_ doesn't attempt new saves.

**Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Failure** The target is fascinated by the cloud.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is fascinated by the cloud. While it remains fascinated, it can't use reactions.

</Spell>
