As part of casting agile feet, you can Step, Stride, or [Tumble Through](/Actions.aspx?ID=29); you can instead [Burrow](/Actions.aspx?ID=93), [Climb](/Actions.aspx?ID=33), [Fly](/Actions.aspx?ID=94), or [Swim](/Actions.aspx?ID=39) if you have the appropriate Speed.
