<Spell title={"Monstrosity Form"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Polymorph']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={8} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1608"} duration={60} source={['Player Core pg. 344']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
You transform into the shape of a legendary monster, assuming a Huge battle form. When you Cast this Spell, choose phoenix, cave worm, or sea serpent. While in this form, you gain the [beast](/Traits.aspx?ID=20) trait (for phoenix) or the [animal](/Traits.aspx?ID=9) trait (for cave worm or sea serpent). You can Dismiss the spell.

 You gain the following statistics and abilities regardless of which battle form you choose: 
<ul><li>AC = 20 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.</li><li>20 temporary Hit Points.</li><li>[Darkvision](/Rules.aspx?ID=415).</li><li>One or more [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) melee attacks specific to the battle form you choose, which are the only attacks you can Strike with. You're trained with them. Your attack modifier is +28, and you use the listed damage. These attacks are Strength based (for the purpose of the [enfeebled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=13) condition, for example). If your unarmed attack modifier is higher, you can use it instead.</li><li>[Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) modifier of +30, unless your own is higher.</li></ul> You gain specific abilities based on the monster you choose:

- **Phoenix** Speed 30 feet, fly 90 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> beak ([reach 15 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d6+12 piercing plus 2d4 fire and 2d4 [persistent fire](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29); **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> talon ([agile](/Traits.aspx?ID=170), [reach 15 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d8+12 slashing; **Shroud of Flame** ([aura](/Traits.aspx?ID=206), [fire](/Traits.aspx?ID=72), [primal](/Traits.aspx?ID=134)) 20 feet. You gain an aura of fire that extends out from you. A creature that enters or ends its turn within the aura takes 2d6 fire damage. A creature can take this damage only once per turn. You can activate or deactivate this aura with a Sustain action.
- **Cave Worm** Speed 40 feet, burrow 30 feet, swim 20 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> jaws ([reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d12+20 piercing; Melee <actions string="Single Action" /> stinger ([agile](/Traits.aspx?ID=170), [reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), Damage 2d8+15 piercing plus 2d6 [persistent poison](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29); Melee <actions string="Single Action" /> body ([reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)) Damage 2d8+20 bludgeoning; **Inexorable** You automatically recover from the [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28), [slowed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35), and [stunned](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36) conditions at the end of each of your turns. You're also immune to being [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24) and ignore [difficult terrain and greater difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453).
- **Sea Serpent** Speed 20 feet, swim 90 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> jaws ([reach 15 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d12+20 piercing; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tail ([reach 25 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d8+20 bludgeoning; **Spine Rake** <actions string="Two Actions" /> ([move](/Traits.aspx?ID=114)) You extend your spines and Swim or Stride. Each creature you're adjacent to at any point during your movement takes 4d8+10 slashing damage ([basic](/Rules.aspx?ID=327) Reflex against your spell DC).

---
**Heightened (9th)** You instead gain AC = 22 + your level, 25 temporary HP, attack modifier +31, increase damage by one damage die, and Athletics +33.
