**Critical Success** You are confused for 1 round.<br />
**Success** At the start of your next turn, you spend your first action with the confused condition, then act normally.<br />
**Failure** The target spends the first action of each of its turns confused. The duration is 1 minute.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is confused. The duration is 1 minute.
