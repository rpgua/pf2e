A wave of magical light washes over the area. You choose the appearance of the light, such as colorful, heatless flames or sparkling motes. A creature affected by revealing light is [dazzled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=7). If the creature was [invisible](/Conditions.aspx?ID=26), it becomes [concealed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=4) instead. If the creature was already concealed for any other reason, it is no longer concealed.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The light affects the creature for 2 rounds.<br />
**Failure** The light affects the creature for 1 minute.<br />
**Critical Failure** The light affects the creature for 10 minutes.

</Spell>
