<Spell title={"Pummeling Rubble"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Earth'], ['Manipulate']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1642"} area={"15-foot cone"} element={['Earth']} saving_throw={"Reflex"} source={['Player Core pg. 351']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
A spray of heavy rocks flies through the air in front of you. The rubble deals 2d4 bludgeoning damage to each creature in the area. Each creature must attempt a Reflex save.

**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The creature takes half damage.<br />
**Failure** The creature takes full damage and is [pushed](/Rules.aspx?ID=451) 5 feet away from you.<br />
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and is pushed 10 feet away from you.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d4.

</Spell>
