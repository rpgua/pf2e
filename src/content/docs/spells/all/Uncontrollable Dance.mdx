<Spell title={"Uncontrollable Dance"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Incapacitation'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={8} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1730"} range={0} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 364']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
The target is overcome with an all-consuming urge to dance. For the duration of the spell, the target is [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) and can't use reactions. It also can't use [move](/Traits.aspx?ID=114) actions except to dance, using the Stride action to move up to half its Speed.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The spell's duration is 3 rounds, and the target must spend at least 1 action each turn dancing.<br />
**Failure** The spell's duration is 1 minute, and the target must spend at least 2 actions each turn dancing.<br />
**Critical Failure** The spell's duration is 1 minute, and the target must spend all its actions each turn dancing.

</Spell>
