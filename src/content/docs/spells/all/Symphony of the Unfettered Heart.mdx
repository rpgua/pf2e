Your symphony lifts listeners from their worldly concerns. Attempt a [Performance](/Skills.aspx?ID=12) check to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) an effect applying one of the following conditions to the target: [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20), [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24), [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28), [restrained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=33), [slowed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35), or [stunned](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36). If you fail, you can't target the same effect on the target for 1 day. Use the condition's source to determine the counteract DC (for example, the [Escape](/Actions.aspx?ID=79) DC for grabbed).

---
**Heightened (9th)** You can target up to four creatures.
