You shout a command that's hard to ignore. You can command the target to approach you, run away (as if it had the [fleeing](/Conditions.aspx?ID=17) condition), release what it's holding, drop [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31), or stand in place. It can't [Delay](/Actions.aspx?ID=77) or take any reactions until it has obeyed your command. The effects depend on the target's Will save.

**Success** The creature is unaffected.<br />
**Failure** The creature must spend the first action on its next turn to obey your command.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target must use all its actions on its next turn to obey your command.

---
**Heightened (5th)** You can target up to 10 creatures.

</Spell>
