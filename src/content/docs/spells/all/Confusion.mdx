<Spell title={"Confusion"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Emotion'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1471"} duration={60} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 321']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
You befuddle your target with strange impulses, causing it to act randomly. The effects are determined by the target's Will save. You can Dismiss the spell.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target babbles incoherently and is [stunned 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36).<br />
**Failure** The target is [confused](/Conditions.aspx?ID=5) for 1 minute. It can attempt a new save at the end of each of its turns to end the confusion.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is confused for 1 minute, with no save to end early.

---
**Heightened (8th)** You can target up to 10 creatures.

</Spell>
