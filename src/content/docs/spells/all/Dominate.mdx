<Spell title={"Dominate"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Concentrate'], ['Incapacitation'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1501"} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 326']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Divine', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
You take command of the target, forcing it to obey you. If you issue an obviously self-destructive order, the target doesn't act until you issue a new order. The effect depends on its Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is stunned 1 as it fights off your commands.<br />
**Failure** You control the target. It gains the [controlled](/Actions.aspx?ID=2175) condition, but it can attempt a Will save at the end of each of its turns. On a success, the spell ends.<br />
**Critical Failure** As a failure, but the target receives a new save only if you give it a new order that is against its nature, such as killing its allies.

---
**Heightened (10th)** The duration is unlimited.

</Spell>
