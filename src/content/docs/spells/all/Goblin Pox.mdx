Your touch afflicts the target with goblin pox, an irritating allergenic rash. The target must attempt a Fortitude save. 

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is [sickened 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=34).<br />
**Failure** The target is afflicted with goblin pox at stage 1.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is afflicted with goblin pox at stage 2. 

**Goblin Pox** ([disease](/Traits.aspx?ID=46)) Level 1; Creatures that have the [goblin](/Traits.aspx?ID=81) trait and [goblin dogs](/Monsters.aspx?ID=236) are immune; **Stage 1** sickened 1 (1 round); **Stage 2** sickened 1 and [slowed 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35) (1 round); **Stage 3** sickened 1 and the creature can't reduce its sickened value below 1 (1 day)

</Spell>
