A black tracer bolt flies toward your target, and upon making contact intensifies into a powerful destructive beam. Make a spell attack against the target. If you hit an object or force construct (such as a [_wall of force_](/Spells.aspx?ID=363)), it's destroyed with no save unless it's an [artifact](/Traits.aspx?ID=253) or similarly powerful. A single casting can destroy no more than a 10-foot cube of matter. 

If you hit a creature, it takes 12d10 damage (no damage type) with a basic Fortitude save. If you critically hit, the target gets a result one degree of success worse than the outcome of its Fortitude save. A creature reduced to 0 HP is blasted to fine powder; its gear remains.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d10.
