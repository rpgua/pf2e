You reach within for a different part of yourself, and you set it free, transforming your body into another form. You polymorph into any form listed in [_pest form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=217), which lasts 10 minutes. All other _untamed form_ shapes last 1 minute. You can add more shapes to your _untamed form_ list with druid feats; your feat might grant  you some or all of the shapes from a given [polymorph](/Traits.aspx?ID=127) spell. When you transform into a shape granted by a spell, you gain all the effects of the shape you chose from a version of the spell heightened to _untamed form'_s rank. _Untamed form_ allows you to use your own shapeshifting training more easily than most polymorph spells. When you choose to use your own attack modifier while polymorphed instead of the form's default attack modifier, you gain a +2 status bonus to your attack rolls.

---
**Heightened (2nd)** You can transform into shapes listed in [_animal form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=10).

</Spell>
