You make the target easier to open. _Knock_ grants a +4 status bonus to any creature that tries to open the target door, lock, or container with an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) or a [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17) check. You can attempt a Thievery check to open the target as part of casting _knock_, and you add your level even if you're untrained.

 _Knock_ [counteracts](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) _lock_.

</Spell>
