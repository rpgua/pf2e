You free the target's limbs from ailments that impede mobility. Attempt to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) an effect of your choice imposing one of these conditions on the target: [clumsy](/Conditions.aspx?ID=3), [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20), or [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28). If you didn't counteract the effect, but you would have if its counteract rank were 2 lower, instead suppress the effect until the beginning of your next turn. The effect's duration doesn't elapse while it's suppressed.

 This spell can't counteract or suppress [curses](/Traits.aspx?ID=38), [diseases](/Traits.aspx?ID=46), or conditions that are part of the target's normal state.

---
**Heightened (4th)** Add [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24), [slowed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35) and [restrained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=33) to the list of conditions.

**Heightened (6th)** Add [petrified](/Conditions.aspx?ID=30) to the list of conditions.

**Heightened (8th)** Add [stunned](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36) to the list of conditions.

</Spell>
