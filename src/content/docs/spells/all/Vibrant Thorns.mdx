Your body sprouts brambly thorns that thrive on life magic. Adjacent creatures that hit you with a melee attack, as well as creatures that hit you with [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) attacks, take 1 piercing damage each time they do. Anytime you cast a [vitality](/Traits.aspx?ID=509) spell, the thorns' damage increases to 1d6 until the start of your next turn.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1, or 1d6 after you cast a vitality spell.
