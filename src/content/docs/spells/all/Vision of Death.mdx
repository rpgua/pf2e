<Spell title={"Vision of Death"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Death'], ['Emotion'], ['Fear'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1742"} deity={['Norgorber', 'Pharasma']} legacy_name={"Phantasmal Killer"} range={120} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 366']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 living creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
You force the target to see a vision of its own death. It takes 8d6 mental damage with a Will save. If the target is reduced to 0 HP by this spell, its vision becomes reality and kills it instantly.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target takes half damage and is [frightened 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=19).<br />
**Failure** The target takes full damage and is frightened 2.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target takes double damage, is frightened 4, and is [fleeing](/Conditions.aspx?ID=17) for as long as it's frightened.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d6.

</Spell>
