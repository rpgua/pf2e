The first time each round you Sustain the spell, you must choose a new target to be subject to the same effect; the same creature can never be targeted more than once with a single casting of this spell. You also can't affect more than one creature per turn with _implosion_. You can't target a creature that's [incorporeal](/Traits.aspx?ID=222), gaseous, or liquid, or otherwise lacking a solid form.
