<Spell title={"Mind Reading"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Concentrate'], ['Detection'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={3} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1602"} duration={6} range={30} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 343']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
With a cursory mental touch, you attempt to read the target's mind. It must attempt a Will save. The target then becomes temporarily immune to your _mind reading_ for 1 hour.

**Critical Success** The target perceives vague surface thoughts from you when you Cast the Spell.<br />
**Success** You find out whether the target's Intelligence modifier is higher than, equal to, or lower than yours.<br />
**Failure** You perceive vague surface thoughts from the target when you Cast the Spell, and you find out whether its Intelligence is higher than, equal to, or lower than yours.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, and for the duration of the spell, you can Sustain the spell to detect the target's surface thoughts again. The target doesn't receive any additional saves.

</Spell>
