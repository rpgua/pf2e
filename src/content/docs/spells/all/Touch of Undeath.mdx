<Spell title={"Touch of Undeath"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Focus'], ['Manipulate'], ['Void']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1846"} domain={['Undeath']} range={0} saving_throw={"Fortitude"} source={['Player Core pg. 380']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 living creature"} type={"Spell"}>
You attack the target's life force with undeath, dealing 1d6 void damage. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target takes half damage.<br />
**Failure** The target takes full damage, and [vitality](/Traits.aspx?ID=509) effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 round.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target takes double damage, and vitality effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 minute.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d6.

</Spell>
