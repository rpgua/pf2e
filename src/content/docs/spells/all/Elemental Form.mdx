<Spell title={"Elemental Form"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Polymorph']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1510"} duration={60} source={['Player Core pg. 328']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
You call upon the power of the planes to transform into a Medium elemental battle form. When you Cast this Spell, choose a listed element. While in this form, you gain the corresponding trait and the [elemental](/Traits.aspx?ID=57) trait. You have hands in this battle form and can take [manipulate](/Traits.aspx?ID=104) actions. You can Dismiss the spell. 

You gain the following statistics and abilities regardless of which battle form you choose: 
<ul><li>AC = 19 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.</li><li>10 temporary Hit Points.</li><li>[Darkvision](/Rules.aspx?ID=415).</li><li>One or more [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) melee attacks specific to the battle form you choose, which are the only attacks you can Strike with. You're trained with them. Your attack modifier is +18, and your damage bonus is +9. These are Dexterity based (air, fire, or metal) or Strength based (earth, water, or wood). If your corresponding unarmed attack modifier is higher, you can use it instead.</li><li>[Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) (air, fire, or metal) or [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) (earth, water, or wood) modifier of +20; ignore this change if your own modifier is higher.</li></ul> You gain specific abilities based on the element you choose:

- **Air** fly 80 feet, movement doesn't trigger reactions; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> gust, **Damage** 1d4 bludgeoning.
- **Earth** Speed 20 feet, burrow 20 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> boulder, **Damage** 2d10 bludgeoning.
- **Fire** Speed 50 feet; fire resistance 10, weakness 5 to cold and 5 to water; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tendril, **Damage** 1d8 fire plus 1d4 [persistent fire](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29).
- **Metal** Speed 40 feet, fly 20 feet; Melee <actions string="Single Action" /> blade ([versatile piercing](/Traits.aspx?ID=200)), Damage 1d8 slashing plus 1d4 electricity.
- **Water** Speed 20 feet, swim 60 feet; fire resistance 5; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> wave, **Damage** 1d12 bludgeoning, and you can spend an action immediately after a hit to push the target 5 feet with the effects of a successful [Shove](/Actions.aspx?ID=38).
- **Wood** Speed 20 feet, climb 30 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> branch, **Damage** 2d10 bludgeoning.

---
**Heightened (6th)** Your battle form is Large and your attacks have 10-foot reach. You instead gain AC = 22 + your level, 15 temporary HP, an attack modifier of +23, a damage bonus of +13, and Acrobatics or Athletics +23.

**Heightened (7th)** Your battle form is Huge and your attacks have 15-foot reach. You instead gain AC = 22 + your level, 20 temporary HP, an attack modifier of +25, a damage bonus of +11, double the number of damage dice (including persistent damage), and Acrobatics or Athletics +25.

</Spell>
