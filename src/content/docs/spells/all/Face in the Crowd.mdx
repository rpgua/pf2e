<Spell title={"Face in the Crowd"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Focus'], ['Manipulate'], ['Visual']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1784"} domain={['Cities']} duration={60} source={['Player Core pg. 372']} spell_type={"Focus"} type={"Spell"}>
While in a crowd of roughly similar creatures, your appearance becomes bland and nondescript. You gain a +2 status bonus to [Deception](/Skills.aspx?ID=5) and [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) checks to go incognito among the crowd, and you ignore [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453) caused by the crowd. This counts as setting up a disguise for the [Impersonate](/Actions.aspx?ID=46) use of Deception and you add your level even if you're untrained.

---
**Heightened (3rd)** The spell gains a range of 10 feet and can target up to 10 creatures.

</Spell>
