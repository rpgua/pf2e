<Spell title={"Overstuff"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Concentrate'], ['Focus'], ['Manipulate']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1810"} domain={['Indulgence']} range={30} saving_throw={"Fortitude"} source={['Player Core pg. 376']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 living creature"} type={"Spell"}>
Huge amounts of food and drink fill the target. It receives a full meal's worth of nourishment and must attempt a Fortitude save. A target [sickened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=34) by this spell takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speed until it's no longer sickened.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is sickened 1, but if it spends an action to end the condition, it succeeds automatically.<br />
**Failure** The target is sickened 1.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is sickened 2.

</Spell>
