<Spell title={"Touch of the Moon"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Focus'], ['Light'], ['Manipulate']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1821"} domain={['Moon']} duration={60} range={0} source={['Player Core pg. 377']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 creature"} type={"Spell"}>
When you touch the target, a symbol of the moon appears on its forehead, glowing with soft moonlight. The target glows with [dim light](/Rules.aspx?ID=408) in a 20-foot radius. It also gets a benefit based on a phase of the moon, starting with the new moon and changing to the next phase at the end of each of its turns. 
<ul><li>**New Moon** The target receives no benefit.</li><li>**Waxing Moon** The target gains a +1 status bonus to attack rolls and a +4 status bonus to damage rolls.</li><li>**Full Moon** The target gains a +1 status bonus to attack rolls, AC, and saves, and a +4 status bonus to damage.</li><li>**Waning Moon** The target gains a +1 status bonus to AC and saving throws. After this phase, return to the new moon.</li></ul>

</Spell>
