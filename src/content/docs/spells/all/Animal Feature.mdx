Without fully transforming your body, you gain one animalistic feature, which you select from the list below each time you Cast the Spell. 
<ul><li>**Cat Eyes** You gain [low-light vision](/Rules.aspx?ID=416).</li><li>**Claws** You gain a claw attack that deals 1d6 slashing damage and has the [agile](/Traits.aspx?ID=170), [finesse](/Traits.aspx?ID=179), and [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) traits.</li><li>**Jaws** You gain a jaws attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage and has the [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) trait.</li></ul>

---
**Heightened (4th)** Add the following options to the list. 
<ul><li>**Fish Tail** You gain a swim Speed equal to your land Speed.</li><li>**Owl Eyes** You gain [darkvision](/Rules.aspx?ID=415).</li><li>**Wings** You gain a fly Speed equal to your land Speed.</li></ul>

</Spell>
