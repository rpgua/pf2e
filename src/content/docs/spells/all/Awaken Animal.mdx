**Critical Success** The target's Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma modifiers each increase to +2 if they were lower, and it becomes [helpful](/Conditions.aspx?ID=21) to you for awakening it.<br />
**Success** The target's Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma modifiers increase to +0 if they were lower and it becomes [friendly](/Conditions.aspx?ID=18) to you for awakening it.<br />
**Failure** You fail to awaken the target.<br />
**Critical Failure** You accidentally awaken the target with a pure bestial hatred toward you. The target's Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma modifiers increase to –2 if they were lower. It becomes [hostile](/Conditions.aspx?ID=23) to you, attempting to destroy you.
