<Spell title={"Phantasmal Calamity"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Illusion'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1630"} area={"30-foot burst"} range={500} saving_throw={"Will"} school={"illusion"} source={['Player Core pg. 349']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
A vision of apocalyptic destruction fills the mind of each creature in the area. The vision deals 11d6 mental damage ([basic](/Rules.aspx?ID=329) Will save). On a critical failure, the creature must also succeed at a Reflex save or believe it's trapped (stuck in a fissure, adrift at sea, or some other fate in keeping with its vision). If it fails the second save, it's also [stunned](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36) for 1 minute. It can attempt a new Will save at the end of each of its turns, and on a success, it disbelieves the illusion and recovers from the stunned condition.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 2d6.

</Spell>
