You form an [invisible](/Conditions.aspx?ID=26) wall of pure magical force up to 50 feet long and up to 20 feet high. The wall has no discernible thickness. You must create the wall in an unbroken open space so its edges don't pass through any creatures or objects, or the spell is lost. The wall has AC 10, Hardness 30, and 60 Hit Points, and it's immune to critical hits and precision damage. The wall blocks physical effects from passing through it, and because it's made of force, it blocks [incorporeal](/Traits.aspx?ID=222) and [ethereal](/Equipment.aspx?ID=284) creatures as well. [Teleportation](/Traits.aspx?ID=156) effects can pass through the barrier, as can [visual](/Traits.aspx?ID=163) effects (since the wall is invisible).

 _Wall of force_ is immune to effects of its rank or lower that attempt to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) it. The wall is automatically destroyed by a [_disintegrate_](/Spells.aspx?ID=76) spell of any rank.

---
**Heightened (+2)** The Hit Points of the wall increases by 20.
