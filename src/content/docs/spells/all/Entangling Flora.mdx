Plants and fungi burst out or quickly grow, entangling creatures. All surfaces in the area are [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453). Each round that a creature starts its turn in the area, it must attempt a Reflex save. On a failure, it takes a –10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds until it leaves the area, and on a critical failure, it's also [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24) for 1 round. Creatures can attempt to [Escape](/Actions.aspx?ID=79) to remove these effects.
