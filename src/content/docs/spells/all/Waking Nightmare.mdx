You fill the creature's mind with a terrifying vision. The target must attempt a Will save. A creature [frightened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=19) by this spell takes 1 additional mental damage each time it's hit by a Strike.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is frightened 1.<br />
**Failure** The target is frightened 2. If it's asleep, it wakes up and is [paralyzed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=28) for 1 round.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, but frightened 3.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The mental damage increases by 1.
