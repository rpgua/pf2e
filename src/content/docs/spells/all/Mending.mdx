You repair the target item. You restore 5 Hit Points per spell rank to the target, potentially removing the [broken](/Conditions.aspx?ID=2) condition if this repairs it past the item's Broken Threshold. You can't replace lost pieces or repair an object that's been completely destroyed.

---
**Heightened (2nd)** You can target a non-magical object of 1 Bulk or less.

**Heightened (3rd)** You can target a non-magical object of 2 Bulk or less, or a magical object of 1 Bulk or less.
