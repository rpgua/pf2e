<Spell title={"Tree of Seasons"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Plant'], ['Wood']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1725"} duration={60} element={['Wood']} legacy_name={"Fire Seeds"} range={60} source={['Player Core pg. 364']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
You cause a Small tree to instantly sprout in an unoccupied space on the ground. Four seedpods grow from the tree, each filled with the magic of a different one of the four seasons. A creature can Interact to pluck one of the pods, and can then either throw it up to 30 feet as part of the same action or do so with a separate Interact action later. When thrown, a pod explodes in a 5-foot burst, dealing 6d6 damage with a [basic](/Rules.aspx?ID=329) Reflex save against your spell DC. The damage type depends on the season of the pod: electricity for spring, fire for summer, poison for autumn, or cold for winter. When the spell ends, the tree withers away and any remaining pods rot, leaving behind non-magical seeds.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The burst's damage increases by 1d6.

</Spell>
