Divine grace ensure that nothing can keep you prisoner or hold you back. You immediately escape from every magical effect that has you [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24) or [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20) unless the effect is of a higher rank than your _unimpeded stride_ spell. You then Stride. During this movement, you ignore [difficult terrain](/Rules.aspx?ID=453) and any circumstance or status penalties to your Speed.
