You send a surge of healing energy to restore the target's body. Attempt to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) an effect of your choice imposing one of these conditions on the target: [blinded](/Conditions.aspx?ID=1), [dazzled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=7), [deafened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=8), [enfeebled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=13), or [sickened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=34). If you didn't counteract the effect, but you would have if its counteract rank were 2 lower, instead suppress the effect until the beginning of your next turn. The effect's duration doesn't elapse while it's suppressed. This spell can't counteract or suppress [curses](/Traits.aspx?ID=38), [diseases](/Traits.aspx?ID=46), or conditions that are part of the target's normal state.

---
**Heightened (4th)** Add [drained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=10) and [slowed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=35) to the list of conditions.

**Heightened (8th)** Add [stunned](/Conditions.aspx?ID=36) to the list of conditions.

</Spell>
