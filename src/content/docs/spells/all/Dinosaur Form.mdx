<Spell title={"Dinosaur Form"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Polymorph']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={4} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1489"} duration={60} source={['Player Core pg. 324']} spell_type={"Spell"} tradition={['Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
You channel the primal forces of nature to transform into a Large animal battle form, specifically that of a powerful and terrifying dinosaur. When you Cast this Spell, choose a listed battle form. You can decide the specific type of animal, but this has no effect on the form's Size or statistics. While in this form, you gain the [animal](/Traits.aspx?ID=9) and [dinosaur](/Traits.aspx?ID=45) traits. You can Dismiss the spell.

 You gain the following statistics and abilities regardless of which battle form you choose: 
<ul><li>AC = 18 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.</li><li>15 temporary Hit Points.</li><li>Low-light vision and imprecise scent 30 feet.</li><li>One or more [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) melee attacks specific to the battle form you choose, which are the only attacks you can Strike with. You're trained with them. Your attack modifier is +16, and your damage bonus is +9. These attacks are Strength based (for the purpose of the [enfeebled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=13) condition, for example). If your unarmed attack modifier is higher, you can use it instead.</li><li>[Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) modifier of +18, unless your own is higher.</li></ul> You also gain specific abilities based on the form you choose:

- **Ankylosaurus** Speed 25 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tail ([backswing](/Traits.aspx?ID=173), [reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d6 bludgeoning; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> foot, **Damage** 2d6 bludgeoning.
- **Brontosaurus** Speed 25 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tail ([reach 15 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d6 bludgeoning; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> foot, **Damage** 2d8 bludgeoning.
- **Deinonychus** Speed 40 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> talon ([agile](/Traits.aspx?ID=170)), **Damage** 2d4 piercing plus 1 [persistent bleed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29); **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> jaws, **Damage** 1d10 piercing.
- **Stegosaurus** Speed 30 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tail ([reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 2d8 piercing.
- **Triceratops** Speed 30 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> horn, **Damage** 2d8 piercing, plus 1d6 [persistent bleed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29) on a critical hit; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> foot, **Damage** 2d6 bludgeoning.
- **Tyrannosaurus** Speed 30 feet; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> jaws ([deadly d12](/Traits.aspx?ID=174), [reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 1d12 piercing; **Melee** <actions string="Single Action" /> tail ([reach 10 feet](/Traits.aspx?ID=192)), **Damage** 1d10 bludgeoning.

---
**Heightened (5th)** Your battle form is Huge and your attacks have 15-foot reach, or 20-foot reach if they started with 15-foot reach. You instead gain 20 temporary HP, an attack modifier of +18, a damage bonus of +6, double the damage dice, and Athletics +21.

**Heightened (7th)** Your battle form is Gargantuan and your attacks have 20-foot reach, or 25-foot reach if they started with 15-foot reach. You instead gain AC = 21 + your level, 25 temporary HP, an attack modifier of +25, a damage bonus of +15, double the damage dice, and Athletics +25.

</Spell>
