<Spell title={"Quandary"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Extradimensional'], ['Manipulate'], ['Teleportation']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={8} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1644"} deity={['Nethys']} legacy_name={"Maze"} range={30} source={['Player Core pg. 351']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Occult']} type={"Spell"}>
You transport the target into an extraplanar puzzle room of mysterious origin, locking them there. Once each turn as a single action, the target can attempt an [Occultism](/Skills.aspx?ID=11) check,  Perception check, or [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17) check against your spell DC to solve the puzzle. [Teleportation](/Traits.aspx?ID=156) effects can't carry the target outside the puzzle room unless they can also traverse the planes, such as [_interplanar teleport_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1576). When the spell ends, the target returns to the space it occupied when it was banished, or to the nearest space if the original is now filled.

**Critical Success** The target solves the puzzle and escapes.<br />
**Success** The target is on the right path to the solution. If it was already on the right path, it solves the puzzle and escapes.<br />
**Failure** The target makes no progress toward a solution.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target makes no progress and, if it was on the right path, it no longer is.

</Spell>
