You shine a blazing ray of light tinged with holy energy. Make a ranged spell attack. The ray deals 5d6 fire damage. If the target has the unholy trait, you deal an extra 5d6 spirit damage. <br />

**Critical Success** The target takes double damage.<br />
**Success** The target takes full damage.

 If the light passes through an area of magical [darkness](/Traits.aspx?ID=39) or targets a creature affected by magical darkness, _holy light_ attempts to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) the darkness. If you need to determine whether the light passes through an area of darkness, draw a line between yourself and the spell's target.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The fire damage increases by 2d6, and the spirit damage against unholy creatures increases by 2d6.

</Spell>
