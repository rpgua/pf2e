Your patron wraps a hand around your target's heart. The target must attempt a Fortitude saving throw. Regardless of the result, the target is temporarily immune to all _curses of death_ for 1 day. 

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target is afflicted with the curse of death at stage 1, and the stage of the curse can't increase beyond stage 1.<br />
**Failure** The target is afflicted with the curse of death at stage 1.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target is afflicted with the curse of death at stage 2. **Curse of Death** ([curse](/Traits.aspx?ID=38), [death](/Traits.aspx?ID=40), [void](/Traits.aspx?ID=510)) This curse ends when the spell ends; **Stage 1** 4d6 void damage and [fatigued](/Conditions.aspx?ID=15) (1 round); **Stage 2** 8d6 void damage and fatigued (1 round); **Stage 3** 12d6 void damage and fatigued (1 round); **Stage 4** death

---
**Heightened (+1)** Increase the void damage taken on a success and during the first three stages of the curse by 1d6.
