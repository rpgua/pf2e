<Spell title={"Earthbind"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Earth'], ['Manipulate']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={3} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1506"} deity={['Torag']} element={['Earth']} range={120} saving_throw={"Fortitude"} source={['Player Core pg. 327']} spell_type={"Spell"} target={"1 flying creature"} tradition={['Arcane', 'Primal']} type={"Spell"}>
Using the weight of earth, you hamper a target's flight, with effects based on its Fortitude save. If the creature reaches the ground safely, it doesn't take [falling damage](/Rules.aspx?ID=402).

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target falls safely up to 120 feet.<br />
**Failure** The target falls safely up to 120 feet. If it hits the ground, it can't [Fly](/Actions.aspx?ID=94), [_levitate_](/Spells.aspx?ID=170), or otherwise leave the ground for 1 round.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target falls safely up to 120 feet. If it hits the ground, it can't Fly, _levitate_, or otherwise leave the ground for 1 minute.

</Spell>
