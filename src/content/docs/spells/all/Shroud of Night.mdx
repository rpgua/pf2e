Your patron blankets the target's eyes in darkness. If you cast this hex on a willing ally (for instance, one with [light blindness](/MonsterAbilities.aspx?ID=22)), the ally can choose which result it gets without rolling.

**Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Failure** The target is shrouded in murky darkness. It treats [bright light](/Rules.aspx?ID=407) as [dim light](/Rules.aspx?ID=408), and unless it has [greater darkvision](/MonsterAbilities.aspx?ID=12), all creatures are [concealed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=4) to it.

</Spell>
