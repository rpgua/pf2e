You stitch the target to its current plane. While the target is affected by _planar tether_, the spell attempts to [counteract](/Rules.aspx?ID=371) any [teleportation](/Traits.aspx?ID=156) effect that would move the target, or any effect that would transport it to a different plane. _Planar tether's_ duration is determined by the target's Will save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The duration is 1 minute.<br />
**Failure** The duration is 10 minutes.<br />
**Critical Failure** The duration is 1 hour.
