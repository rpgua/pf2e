<Spell title={"Savor the Sting"} traits={[['Uncommon', 3], ['Cleric'], ['Focus'], ['Manipulate'], ['Mental'], ['Nonlethal']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1826"} domain={['Pain']} range={0} saving_throw={"Will"} source={['Player Core pg. 378']} spell_type={"Focus"} target={"1 creature"} type={"Spell"}>
You inflict pain upon the target and revel in their anguish. This deals 1d4 mental damage and 1d4 [persistent mental damage](/Conditions.aspx?ID=29); the target must attempt a Will save. As long as the target is taking persistent damage from this spell, you gain a +1 status bonus to attack rolls and skill checks against the target.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** The target takes half damage and no persistent damage.<br />
**Failure** The target takes full initial and persistent damage.<br />
**Critical Failure** The target takes double initial and persistent damage.

---
**Heightened (+1)** The initial damage increases by 1d4 and the persistent damage increases by 1d4.

</Spell>
