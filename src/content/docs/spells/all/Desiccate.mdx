You pull the moisture from the targets' bodies, dealing 10d10 void damage. Creatures made of water (such as [water elementals](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=45)) and [plant](/Traits.aspx?ID=125) creatures use the outcome for one degree of success worse than the result of their saving throw. Creatures whose bodies contain no significant moisture (such as [earth elementals](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=42)) are immune to _desiccate_.

**Heightened (+1)** The damage increases by 1d10.
