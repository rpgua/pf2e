Your patron's majesty—or their displeasure—comes in a growl from your throat, making other creatures reluctant to harm you. The target must attempt a Will save; if the creature is an [animal](/Traits.aspx?ID=9), [fungus](/Traits.aspx?ID=77), or [plant](/Traits.aspx?ID=125), it takes a –1 circumstance penalty to its save.

**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.<br />
**Success** When the target attempts an attack roll or skill check that would harm you, it takes a –2 status penalty to its roll.<br />
**Failure** As success, but the target also becomes [sickened 1](/Conditions.aspx?ID=34) each time it damages you.<br />
**Critical Failure** As failure, but the sickened value is 2.

</Spell>
