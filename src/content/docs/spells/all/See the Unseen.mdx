Your gaze pierces through illusions and finds [invisible](/Conditions.aspx?ID=26) creatures and spirits. You can see invisible creatures as though they weren't invisible, although their features are blurred, making them concealed and difficult to identify. You can also see [incorporeal](/Traits.aspx?ID=222) creatures, like [ghosts](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=51), phased through an object from within 10 feet of an object's surface as blurry shapes seen through those objects. Subtler clues also grant you a +2 status bonus to checks you make to disbelieve [illusions](/Traits.aspx?ID=92).

---
**Heightened (5th)** This spell has a duration of 8 hours.
