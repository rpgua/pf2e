You called the wilderness home as you found trails and guided travelers. Your wanderlust could have called you to the adventuring life, or perhaps you served as a scout for soldiers and found you liked battle.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Wisdom, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Survival](/Skills.aspx?ID=16) skill, and a [Lore](/skills/lore) skill related to one terrain you scouted in (such as Forest Lore or Cavern Lore). You gain the [Forager](/Feats.aspx?ID=786) skill feat.
