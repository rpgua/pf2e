Creating all sorts of minor inventions scratches your itch for problem-solving. Your engineering skills take a particularly creative bent, and no one know what you’ll come up with next. It might be a genius device with tremendous potential... or it might explode.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Intelligence, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4) skill, and the [Engineering Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Specialty Crafting](/Feats.aspx?ID=844) skill feat.
