The bloody games of the arena taught you the art of combat. Before you attained true fame, you departed—or escaped—the arena to explore the world. Your skill at drawing both blood and a crowd’s attention pay off in a new adventuring life.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Strength
or Charisma, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Performance](/Skills.aspx?ID=12) skill, and the [Gladiatorial Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Impressive Performance](/Feats.aspx?ID=793) skill feat.
