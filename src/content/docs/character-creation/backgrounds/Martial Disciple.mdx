You dedicated yourself to intense training and rigorous study to become a great warrior. The school you attended might have been a traditionalist monastery, an elite military academy, or the local branch of a prestigious mercenary organization.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Strength
or Dexterity, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in your choice of either the [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1)
 or the [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) skill, as well as the [Warfare Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain a skill feat: [Cat Fall](/Feats.aspx?ID=765) if you chose [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) or [Quick Jump](/Feats.aspx?ID=825) if you chose [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3).
