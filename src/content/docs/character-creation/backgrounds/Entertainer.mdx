Through an education in the arts or sheer dogged practice, you learned to entertain crowds. You might have been an actor, a dancer, a musician, a street magician, or any other sort of performer.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Charisma, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Performance](/Skills.aspx?ID=12) skill, and the [Theater Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Fascinating Performance](/Feats.aspx?ID=781) skill feat.
