Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Intelligence
or Wisdom, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Society](/Skills.aspx?ID=14) skill, and the [Underworld Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Streetwise](/Feats.aspx?ID=846) skill feat.
