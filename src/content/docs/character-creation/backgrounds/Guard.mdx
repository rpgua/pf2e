You served in the guard, out of either patriotism or the need for coin. Either way, you know how to get a difficult suspect to talk. However you left the guard, you might think of adventuring as a way to use your skills on a wider stage.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Strength
or Charisma, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Intimidation](/Skills.aspx?ID=7) skill, and the [Legal Lore](/skills/lore) skill or Warfare Lore skill. You gain the [Quick Coercion](/Feats.aspx?ID=822) skill feat.
