Your art is your greatest passion, whatever form it takes. Adventuring might help you find inspiration, or simply be a way to survive until you become a world-famous artist.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Charisma, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4) skill, and the [Art Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Specialty Crafting](/Feats.aspx?ID=844) skill feat.
