You eked out a living by picking pockets on the streets of a major city, never knowing where you’d find your next meal. While some folk adventure for the glory, you do so to survive.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Constitution, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Thievery](/Skills.aspx?ID=17) skill, and a [Lore](/skills/lore) skill for the city you lived in as a street urchin (such as Absalom Lore or Magnimar Lore). You gain the [Pickpocket](/Feats.aspx?ID=818) skill feat.
