Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Constitution
or Charisma, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) skill, and the [Alcohol Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Hobnobber](/Feats.aspx?ID=791) skill feat.
