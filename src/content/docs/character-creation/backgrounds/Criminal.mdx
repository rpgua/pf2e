As an unscrupulous independent or as a member of an underworld organization, you lived a life of crime. You might have become an adventurer to seek redemption, to escape the law, or simply to get access to bigger and better loot.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Dexterity
or Intelligence, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Stealth](/Skills.aspx?ID=15) skill, and the [Underworld Lore](/skills/lore) skill. You gain the [Experienced Smuggler](/Feats.aspx?ID=779) skill feat.
