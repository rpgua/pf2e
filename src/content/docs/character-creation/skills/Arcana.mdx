Arcana measures how much you know about arcane magic and creatures.

<title level="2">Arcana Untrained General Actions</title>

- [Recall Knowledge](/Skills.aspx?ID=24&amp;General=true) about arcane theories; magic traditions; creatures of arcane significance (like [constructs](/Traits.aspx?ID=563), [beasts](/Traits.aspx?ID=547), and [elementals](/Traits.aspx?ID=647)); and the [Astral Plane](/Planes.aspx?ID=8), Elemental Planes, and [the Netherworld](/Planes.aspx?ID=36).

<title level="2">Arcana Trained General Actions</title>

- [Decipher Writing](/Skills.aspx?ID=20&amp;General=true) about arcane theory.

- [Identify Magic](/Skills.aspx?ID=22&amp;General=true), particularly magic of the [arcane](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=1) tradition.

- [Learn a Spell](/Skills.aspx?ID=23&amp;General=true) from the [arcane](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=1) tradition.
