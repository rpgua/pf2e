 <title level="1" right="Skill " pfs="">[Religion](/Skills.aspx?ID=46)</title>

<row gap="tiny">**Source** [Player Core](/Sources.aspx?ID=216) pg. 244</row>

The secrets of deities, dogma, faith, and the realms of divine creatures both sublime and sinister are open to you. You also understand how magic works, though your training imparts a religious slant to that knowledge.

<title level="2">Religion Untrained General Actions</title>

- [Recall Knowledge](/Skills.aspx?ID=24&amp;General=true) about divine agents, the finer points of theology, obscure myths regarding a faith, and creatures of religious significance (like [celestials](/Traits.aspx?ID=551), [fiends](/Traits.aspx?ID=600), and [undead](/Traits.aspx?ID=160)), [Creation's Forge](/Planes.aspx?ID=27), [the Void](/Planes.aspx?ID=28), and the Outer Sphere.

<title level="2">Religion Trained General Actions</title>

- [Decipher Writing](/Skills.aspx?ID=20&amp;General=true) of a religious nature, including allegories, homilies, and proverbs.

- [Identify Magic](/Skills.aspx?ID=22&amp;General=true), particularly magic of the [divine](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=2) tradition.

- [Learn a Spell](/Skills.aspx?ID=23&amp;General=true) from the [divine](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=2) tradition.
