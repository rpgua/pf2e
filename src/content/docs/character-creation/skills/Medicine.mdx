<row gap="tiny">**Source** [Player Core](/Sources.aspx?ID=216) pg. 241</row>

You can patch up wounds and help people recover from diseases and poisons. [Treat Wounds](/Actions.aspx?ID=57) is especially useful, allowing your adventuring party to heal up between fights. It can be made more efficient with skill feats like [Continual Recovery](/Feats.aspx?ID=771) and [Ward Medic](/Feats.aspx?ID=864).

<title level="2">Medicine Untrained General Actions</title>

- [Recall Knowledge](/Skills.aspx?ID=24&amp;General=true) about diseases, injuries, poisons, and other ailments. You can use this to perform forensic examinations if you spend 10 minutes (or more, as determined by the GM) checking for evidence such as wound patterns. This is most useful when determining how a body was injured or killed.
