<Feat title={"Bloody Blows"} traits={[['Orc']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4521"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 72

  Your lethal unarmed attacks leave bloody gouges or cause severe internal bleeding. When you critically hit with a [Strike](/Actions.aspx?ID=89) using an unarmed attack that isn’t [nonlethal](/Traits.aspx?ID=188), the target takes 1d4 persistent bleed damage. This can be because you’re taking the penalty to use a fist for a lethal attack or because you have an unarmed attack without the nonlethal trait due to Tusks or a similar ability.
