<Feat title={"Lucky Keepsake"} traits={[['Leshy']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={9} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4505"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 69

 **Prerequisite:** [Leshy Superstition](/Feats.aspx?ID=4496)

 You have a keepsake that grants you luck. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against spells and magical effects at all times, not just when you use [Leshy Superstition](/Feats.aspx?ID=1040). If you lose the keepsake, you lose the bonus until you designate a new keepsake, typically over the course of a week.
