 **Source:** Player Core pg. 79

  You descend from an [angel](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=3), a winged messenger from [Nirvana](/Planes.aspx?ID=20) or one of the other celestial realms. You gain the trained proficiency rank in [Society](/Skills.aspx?ID=14). If you would automatically become trained in Society (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You know the [Empyrean](/Languages.aspx?ID=115) language, and you gain the [Multilingual](/Feats.aspx?ID=814) skill feat.
