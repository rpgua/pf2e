 **Source:** Player Core pg. 77

  You’ve heard the Call, and by resisting it, you’ve developed a bulwark against effects that target your mind. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Will saving throws against [mental](/Traits.aspx?ID=106) effects. If you roll a success on your saving throw against a mental effect that would make you [controlled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=6), you get a critical success instead.
