<Feat title={"Shared Assault"} traits={[['Bard']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={12} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4621"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 106

 **Prerequisite:** [Courageous Assault](/Feats.aspx?ID=4611)

 In the triumph of battle, you can share the glory with another ally. When the ally you chose for [Courageous Assault](/Feats.aspx?ID=4611) critically succeeds at the [Strike](/Actions.aspx?ID=2306) granted by that action, another ally affected by your [_courageous anthem_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1763) [composition](/Traits.aspx?ID=559) spell can immediately use a reaction to make a melee Strike. This effect doesn’t continue to a third ally, even if the second ally also critically succeeds.
