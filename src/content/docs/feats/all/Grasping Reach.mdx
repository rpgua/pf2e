<Feat title={"Grasping Reach"} traits={[['Leshy']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4493"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 68

  You can extend a tangle of vines or tendrils to support your arms and extend your reach. When you wield a melee weapon that requires two hands, doesn’t have [reach](/Traits.aspx?ID=192), and deals at least 1d6 damage, you can change between a typical two-handed grip and an extended two-handed grasp using an [Interact](/Actions.aspx?ID=80) action. Weapons wielded in your extended grasp gain reach of 10 feet. This grasp is less stable and powerful than a typical grip, reducing the weapon’s damage die by 1 step.
