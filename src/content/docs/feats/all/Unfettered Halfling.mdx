<Feat title={"Unfettered Halfling"} traits={[['Halfling']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4463"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 60

  You were either pressed into indentured servitude or shackled in a prison, but you've since escaped and have trained to ensure you'll never be caught again. Whenever you roll a success on a check to [Escape](/Actions.aspx?ID=79) or a saving throw against an effect that would impose the [grabbed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=20), [immobilized](/Conditions.aspx?ID=24), or [restrained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=33) condition on you, you get a critical success instead. Whenever a creature rolls a failure on a check to [Grapple](/Actions.aspx?ID=35) you, it gets a critical failure instead.
