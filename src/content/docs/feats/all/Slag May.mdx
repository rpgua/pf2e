  Your mother was an iron hag known for physical prowess, and you have one violet or steel-gray eye. You have thick, sturdy claws made of cold iron that grow naturally from your body. You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals 1d6 slashing damage. Your claws are in the [brawling](/WeaponGroups.aspx?ID=4) group, have the [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) and [grapple](/Traits.aspx?ID=184) traits, and are [cold iron](/Equipment.aspx?ID=272).
