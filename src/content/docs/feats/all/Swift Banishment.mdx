 **Source:** Player Core pg. 120

 **Trigger:** You critically hit a creature that is not on its home plane

**Requirements:** You have a banishment spell prepared

 The force of your blow sends your victim back to its home plane. You expend a [_banishment_](/Spells.aspx?ID=19) spell you have prepared, affecting the creature you critically hit without needing to cast the spell. The creature can attempt to resist the spell as normal.
