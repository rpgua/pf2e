  Your lineage descends from [devils](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=30), conniving schemers of [Hell](/Planes.aspx?ID=18). You’re as skilled at noticing lies and twisted wordings as you are at constructing them. You are trained in [Deception](/Skills.aspx?ID=5) and Legal [Lore](/Skills.aspx?ID=8). If you were already trained in Deception (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You also gain the [Lie to Me](/Feats.aspx?ID=811) skill feat.
