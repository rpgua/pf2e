<Feat title={"Verdant Metamorphosis"} traits={[['Druid']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={14} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4758"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 135

 **Prerequisite:** [leaf order](/DruidicOrders.aspx?ID=2)

 You have turned into a plant version of yourself, gaining the [plant](/Traits.aspx?ID=125) trait and losing traits inappropriate for your new form (typically [humanoid](/Traits.aspx?ID=91)). You also gain the Verdant Rest action.

**Verdant Rest** <actions string="Single Action" /> ([concentrate](/Traits.aspx?ID=32)) You turn into a tree or other noncreature plant. This has the effect of using [_one with plants_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1618) to turn into a plant, except that your AC is 30. You can [Dismiss](/Actions.aspx?ID=74) this effect to turn back. If you rest for 10 minutes in this form in natural sunlight, you recover half your maximum Hit Points. If you take your daily rest in this form, the rest restores you to maximum Hit Points and removes all non-permanent [drained](/Conditions.aspx?ID=10), [enfeebled](/Conditions.aspx?ID=13), [clumsy](/Conditions.aspx?ID=3), and [stupefied](/Conditions.aspx?ID=37) conditions, as well as all poisons and diseases of 19th level or lower.

 </Feat>
