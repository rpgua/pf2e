<Feat title={"Universal Longevity"} traits={[['Elf']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={13} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4420"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 49

 **Prerequisite:** [Expert Longevity](/Feats.aspx?ID=4416)

**Frequency:** once per day

 You’ve perfected your ability to keep up with all the skills you’ve learned over your long life, so you’re almost never truly untrained at a skill. You reflect on your life experiences, changing the skill you selected with [Ancestral Longevity](/Feats.aspx?ID=4405) (and the skill you selected with [Expert Longevity](/Feats.aspx?ID=4416) if you have that feat).
