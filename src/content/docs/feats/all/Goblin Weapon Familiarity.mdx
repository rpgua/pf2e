<Feat title={"Goblin Weapon Familiarity"} traits={[['Goblin']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4443"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 56

  Others might look upon them with disdain, but you know that the weapons of your people are as effective as they are sharp. You gain access to all [uncommon](/Traits.aspx?ID=159) weapons with the [goblin](/Languages.aspx?ID=6) trait. You have familiarity with weapons with the goblin trait—for the purposes of proficiency, you treat any of these that are martial weapons as simple weapons and any that are advanced weapons as martial weapons.

At 5th level, whenever you get a critical hit with one of these weapons, you get its critical specialization effect.
