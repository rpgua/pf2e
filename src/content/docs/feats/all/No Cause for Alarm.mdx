<Feat title={"No Cause for Alarm"} traits={[['Auditory'], ['Concentrate'], ['Emotion'], ['General'], ['Linguistic'], ['Mental'], ['Skill']]} action={6} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=5184"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 259

 **Prerequisite:** Trained in [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6)

 You attempt to reduce panic. Attempt a Diplomacy check, comparing it to the Will DC of creatures in a 10-foot emanation around you who are frightened. Each of them is temporarily immune for 1 hour.

**Critical Success** Reduce the creature's frightened value by 2.<br />
**Success** Reduce the creature's frightened value by 1.
