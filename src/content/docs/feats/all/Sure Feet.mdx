<Feat title={"Sure Feet"} traits={[['Halfling']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4461"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 60

  Whether keeping your balance or climbing, your feet easily find purchase. If you roll a success on an [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1) check to [Balance](/Actions.aspx?ID=28) or an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check to [Climb](/Actions.aspx?ID=33), you get a critical success instead. You're not [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) when you attempt to Balance or Climb.
