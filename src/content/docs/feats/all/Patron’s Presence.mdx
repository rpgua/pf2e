<Feat title={"Patron’s Presence"} traits={[['Witch']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={14} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=5015"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 190

  Your patron can direct its attention through your familiar, and its mere presence becomes an ominous weight on the minds of other beings to distract them and blot out their magic. Your familiar gains the following activity.

**Patron’s Presence** <actions string="Two Actions" /> (aura) **Frequency** once per hour; **Effect** A palpable weight extends from your familiar in a 15-foot emanation. Enemies who enter or start their turn within the aura must succeed at a Will save against your spell DC or become stupefied 2 as long as they remain within the aura, or stupefied 3 on a critical failure. The aura lasts until the end of your next turn, but the familiar can Sustain it up to 1 minute.
