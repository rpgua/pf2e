<Feat title={"Summon Nephilim Kin"} traits={[['Nephilim']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={13} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4564"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 81

 **Prerequisite:** any [nephilim](/Ancestries.aspx?ID=68) lineage feat

 You have a connection to the [Outer Planes](/Planes.aspx?ID=13), allowing you to summon a divine ally. Choose either [_summon celestial_](/Spells.aspx?ID=317), [_summon fiend_](/Spells.aspx?ID=323), or another 5th-rank spell capable of summoning an extraplanar creature appropriate to your lineage. Once per day, you can cast the chosen spell as a 5th-rank divine [innate spell](/Rules.aspx?ID=279), but the creature summoned must be from the same category as your own lineage, such as a creature with the [angel](/Traits.aspx?ID=8) trait if you are an [angelkin](/Feats.aspx?ID=1348).
