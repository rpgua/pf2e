<Feat title={"Hag Magic"} traits={[['Changeling']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={13} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4541"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 77

  Through your heritage, you can replicate a hag’s magic. Choose one common spell of 4th rank or lower from those available to a coven, including the spells contributed to a coven by a hag of your mother’s type. You can cast this spell once per day as a 4th-rank occult [innate spell](/Rules.aspx?ID=279).

Spells available to all covens are [augury](/Spells.aspx?ID=15), [charm](/Spells.aspx?ID=34), [clairaudience](/Spells.aspx?ID=39), [clairvoyance](/Spells.aspx?ID=40), [dream message](/Spells.aspx?ID=90), and [illusory disguise](/Spells.aspx?ID=159). The eligible spells granted by the most prominent types of hag are: **Cuckoo Hag** [nightmare](/Spells.aspx?ID=208), [spellwrack](/Spells.aspx?ID=298); **Iron Hag** [earthbind](/Spells.aspx?ID=94), _spellwrack_; **Sea Hag** [humanoid form](/Spells.aspx?ID=153), [mariner's curse](/Spells.aspx?ID=184), [water walk](/Spells.aspx?ID=371); **Sweet Hag** _charm_, [_honeyed words_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1558), [outcast's curse](/Spells.aspx?ID=211)

 </Feat>
