<Feat title={"Prairie Rider"} traits={[['Halfling']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4460"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 60

  You grew up riding your clan's shaggy ponies and riding dogs. You become trained in [Nature](/Domains.aspx?ID=22). If you would automatically become trained in Nature (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You also get a +1 circumstance bonus to [Command an Animal](/Actions.aspx?ID=58) if the target is a traditional halfling mount, such as a [pony](/Monsters.aspx?ID=265) or [riding dog](/Monsters.aspx?ID=125).
