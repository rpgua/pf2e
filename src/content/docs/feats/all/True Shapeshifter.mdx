<Feat title={"True Shapeshifter"} traits={[['Concentrate'], ['Druid']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={20} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4767"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 135

 **Prerequisite:** [Dragon Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=4749); [Untamed Form](/Feats.aspx?ID=4713)

 You transcend the limitations of form. Once per day you can cast [_nature incarnate_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1612), even if you don’t have it prepared, to transform into a kaiju. If you have [Plant Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=338), you can choose to transform into a [green man](/Monsters.aspx?ID=1176) instead. You also gain the True Shapeshift activity.

**True Shapeshift** <actions string="Two Actions" /> ([concentrate](/Traits.aspx?ID=32)) **Requirements** You’re under the effects of [untamed form](/Feats.aspx?ID=4713); **Effect** You change into any other shape on your untamed form list. If the duration of that shape is different from the one you were previously in, use the shorter duration to determine your duration remaining.

 </Feat>
