<Feat title={"City Scavenger"} traits={[['Goblin']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4439"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 56

  You know that the greatest treasures often look like refuse. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to checks to [Subsist](/Actions.aspx?ID=27), and you can use [Society](/Skills.aspx?ID=14) or [Survival](/Skills.aspx?ID=16) when you Subsist in a settlement.

When you Subsist in a city, you also gather valuable junk that silly longshanks threw away. You can [Earn Income](/Actions.aspx?ID=23) using Society or Survival at the same time while you Subsist, without spending any additional days of downtime. You also gain a +1 circumstance bonus to this check.

**Special** If you have the irongut goblin heritage, increase the bonuses to +2.
