 **Source:** Player Core pg. 48

  You've studied traditional elven arts, learning about arcane magic and the world around you. You gain the trained proficiency rank in [Arcana](/Skills.aspx?ID=2) and [Nature](/Domains.aspx?ID=22). If you would automatically become trained in one of those skills (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

You also gain the [Additional Lore](/Feats.aspx?ID=750) general feat for Elf [Lore](/Skills.aspx?ID=8).
