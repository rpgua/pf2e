 **Source:** Player Core pg. 52

  You might have a flighty nature, but when a topic captures your attention, you dive into it headfirst. You gain the [Additional Lore](/Feats.aspx?ID=750) feat and the [Assurance](/Feats.aspx?ID=756) feat for the chosen [Lore](/Skills.aspx?ID=8). As gnome obsessions can come and go quickly, you can retrain Gnome Obsession to a different Lore subcategory in 1 day of downtime.
