<Feat title={"Grown of Oak"} traits={[['Druid']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4729"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 131

 **Prerequisite:** [leaf order](/DruidicOrders.aspx?ID=2)

 You can make your skin take on the woody endurance of an ancient tree and have your familiar follow suit. You can cast [_oaken resilience_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1617) at will as an innate primal spell with a spell rank one lower than the highest rank of spells you can cast. This spell targets you, as well as your leshy familiar if it’s within 30 feet of you.
