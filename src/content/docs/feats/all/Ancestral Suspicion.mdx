<Feat title={"Ancestral Suspicion"} traits={[['Elf']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4413"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 48

  Long-lived elves have seen civilizations rise and fall, often at the hands of outside forces. As a result, they have developed a wariness of others who might seek to influence or control them. You've been trained to resist such manipulation, gaining a +2 circumstance bonus to saving throws against effects that would make you controlled, such as [_dominate_](/Spells.aspx?ID=87), and to Perception checks to [Sense Motive](/Actions.aspx?ID=85) when trying to determine if a creature is under the influence of such an effect. When you roll a success on a saving throw against such an effect, you get a critical success instead.
