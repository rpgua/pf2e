<Feat title={"Ceaseless Shadows"} traits={[['Halfling']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={13} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4471"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 61

 **Prerequisite:** [Distracting Shadows](/Feats.aspx?ID=4455)

 You excel at going unnoticed, especially among a crowd. You no longer need to have cover or be [concealed](/Conditions.aspx?ID=4) to [Hide](/Actions.aspx?ID=62) or [Sneak](/Actions.aspx?ID=63). If you would have lesser cover from a creature, you gain standard cover instead and can [Take Cover](/Actions.aspx?ID=90); if you would have standard cover from a creature, you gain greater cover instead.
