<Feat title={"Primal Howl"} traits={[['Druid']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={10} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4745"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 133

 **Prerequisite:** [Incredible Companion (Druid)](/Feats.aspx?ID=4737)

 Your companion can let out a howl laced with your primal magic. It gains the following advanced maneuver, in addition to any advanced maneuvers it already knows. 

**Primal Howl** <actions string="Two Actions" /> ([primal](/Traits.aspx?ID=134), [sonic](/Traits.aspx?ID=147)) **Frequency** once per hour; **Effect** Your familiar screeches and howls, empowered with natural magic. All creatures in a 30-foot cone take 1d6 sonic damage for every 2 levels your companion has, with a basic Fortitude save against your spell DC. Creatures that fail become [frightened](/Conditions.aspx?ID=19) 1, and creatures that critically fail become frightened 2. The fright is an [emotion](/Traits.aspx?ID=60), [fear](/Traits.aspx?ID=68), and [mental](/Traits.aspx?ID=106) effect.

 </Feat>
