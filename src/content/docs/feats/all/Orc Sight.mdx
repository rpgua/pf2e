<Feat title={"Orc Sight"} traits={[['Dromaar']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4572"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 83

 **Prerequisite:** [low-light vision](/Rules.aspx?ID=416)

 Your orc blood grants you the keen vision of your forebears. You gain [darkvision](/Rules.aspx?ID=415), allowing you to see in [darkness](/Rules.aspx?ID=409) and [dim light](/Rules.aspx?ID=408) just as well as you can in [bright light](/Rules.aspx?ID=407). However, in darkness, you see in black and white only.

**Special** You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain out of this feat or into this feat.
