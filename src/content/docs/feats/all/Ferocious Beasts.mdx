<Feat title={"Ferocious Beasts"} traits={[['Orc']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={13} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4527"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 73

 **Prerequisite:** [Bonded Animal](/Feats.aspx?ID=5127), animal companion, or [Pet](/Feats.aspx?ID=5186); [Orc Ferocity](/Feats.aspx?ID=4514)

 Since ancient times, the mightiest orc beast tamers would draw out the true fighting spirit of their companion beasts by feeding the creatures a draft incorporating the orc’s own blood. All [animal companions](/Rules.aspx?ID=149), pets, [familiars](/Rules.aspx?ID=160), and bonded animals you have gain the [Orc Ferocity](/Feats.aspx?ID=83) feat, and gain one reaction per round they can use only for Orc Ferocity. If you have the [Undying Ferocity](/Feats.aspx?ID=1291) ancestry feat, all animal companions, pets, familiars, and bonded animals you have also gain the benefits of that feat when using the Orc Ferocity reaction.

 </Feat>
