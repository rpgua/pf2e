<Feat title={"Bardic Lore"} traits={[['Bard']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4573"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 100

 **Prerequisite:** [enigma muse](/Muses.aspx?ID=1)

 Your studies make you informed on every subject. You are trained in Bardic Lore, a special Lore skill that can be used only to [Recall Knowledge](/Actions.aspx?ID=2367), but on any topic. If you have legendary proficiency in [Occultism](/Skills.aspx?ID=44), you gain expert proficiency in Bardic Lore, but you can’t increase your proficiency rank in Bardic Lore by any other means.
