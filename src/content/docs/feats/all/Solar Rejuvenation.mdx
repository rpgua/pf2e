<Feat title={"Solar Rejuvenation"} traits={[['Leshy']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={9} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4506"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 69

  If you rest outdoors for 10 minutes during the day, you regain Hit Points equal to your Constitution modifier × half your level. You gain this benefit in addition to any healing from [Treat Wounds](/Actions.aspx?ID=57). Leshies whose plant nourishment does not rely on photosynthesis require a similarly suitable environment. For example, [fungus leshies](/Heritages.aspx?ID=45) need dark, damp environments and a pile of decaying plant matter.
