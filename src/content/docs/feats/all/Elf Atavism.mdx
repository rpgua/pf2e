<Feat title={"Elf Atavism"} traits={[['Aiuvarin']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4568"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 82

  Your elven blood runs particularly strong, granting you features far more elven than those of a typical aiuvarin. You may also have been raised among elves, steeped in your elven ancestors’ heritage. You gain the benefits of the elf heritage of your elven parent or ancestors. You typically can’t select a heritage that depends on or improves an elven feature you don’t have. For example, you couldn’t take the [Ancient Elf](/Heritages.aspx?ID=34) heritage unless your non-elf ancestry also has a lifespan measured in multiple centuries. In these cases, at the GM’s discretion, you might gain a different benefit.

**Special** You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain out of this feat or into this feat.
