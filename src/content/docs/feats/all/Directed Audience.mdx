<Feat title={"Directed Audience"} traits={[['Bard']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={2} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4581"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 101

  You can shape the area of your [composition](/Traits.aspx?ID=559) spells. Whenever you cast a composition spell whose area is an [emanation](/Rules.aspx?ID=2387), you can change the spell’s area to a cone 10 feet larger, to a maximum of twice the original area. For example, you could modify a composition spell that affects a 30-foot emanation to affect a 40-foot cone, but a 5-foot emanation could only become a 10-foot cone.
