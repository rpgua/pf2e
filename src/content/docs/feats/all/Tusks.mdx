<Feat title={"Tusks"} traits={[['Orc']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4519"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 72

  You have particularly long, jagged tusks perfect for tearing meat from bone. You gain a tusks unarmed attack that deals 1d6 piercing damage. Your tusks are in the [brawling](/WeaponGroups.aspx?ID=4) group and have the [finesse](/Traits.aspx?ID=179) and [unarmed](/Traits.aspx?ID=199) traits.

**Special** You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain into this feat. You can retrain out of this feat only through drastic measures, such as breaking, filing, or extracting your tusks.

 </Feat>
