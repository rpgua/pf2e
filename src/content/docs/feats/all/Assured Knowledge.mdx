<Feat title={"Assured Knowledge"} traits={[['Bard'], ['Fortune']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={6} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4596"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 103

 **Prerequisite:** [enigma muse](/Muses.aspx?ID=1)

 You can procure information with confidence. Whenever you [Recall Knowledge](/Actions.aspx?ID=2367) using any skill (including [Bardic Lore](/Feats.aspx?ID=4573)), you can forgo rolling your check to instead receive a result of 10 + your proficiency bonus (don’t apply any other bonuses, penalties, or modifiers).

As long as you are an expert in a skill, you meet the prerequisites for the [Automatic Knowledge](/Feats.aspx?ID=5122) skill feat for that skill, even if you don’t have the [Assurance](/Feats.aspx?ID=5121) feat for that skill.
