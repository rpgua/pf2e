<Feat title={"Dwarven Reinforcement"} traits={[['Dwarf']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4396"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 44

 **Prerequisite:** Expert in [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4)

 You can use your knowledge of engineering and metalwork to temporarily strengthen thick objects and structures. By spending 1 hour working on an item, you can give it a +1 circumstance bonus to its Hardness for 24 hours. If you're a master in [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4), the bonus is +2, and if you're legendary, the bonus is +3. You can reinforce a portion of a structure, though 1 hour usually reinforces only a door, a few windows, or another section that fits within a 10-foot cube.
