<Feat title={"Anchoring Roots"} traits={[['Leshy']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4500"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 68

  Small roots sprout from your feet, steadying you as you move. You gain the [Steady Balance](/Feats.aspx?ID=845) skill feat, even if you aren’t trained in [Acrobatics](/Skills.aspx?ID=1), and you can use the Anchor action. 

**Anchor** <actions string="Single Action" /> **Requirements** You’re on a solid surface; **Effect** You send your roots into or across the ground, making it harder for you to stumble. Until you move, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Fortitude or Reflex DC against attempts to [Reposition](/Actions.aspx?ID=2379), [Shove](/Actions.aspx?ID=38), or [Trip](/Actions.aspx?ID=40) you (or a +4 circumstance bonus if you’re a [root leshy](/Heritages.aspx?ID=269)). This bonus also applies to saving throws against spells or effects that attempt to move you or knock you [prone](/Conditions.aspx?ID=31). If an effect forces you to move, you move only half the normal distance, as some of the effort goes to tearing loose the roots.

 </Feat>
