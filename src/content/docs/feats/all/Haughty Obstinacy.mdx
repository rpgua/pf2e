<Feat title={"Haughty Obstinacy"} traits={[['Human']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4477"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 64

  Your powerful ego makes it harder for others to order you around. If you roll a success on a saving throw against a [mental](/Traits.aspx?ID=106) effect that attempts to directly control your actions, you critically succeed instead. If a creature rolls a failure on a check to [Coerce](/Actions.aspx?ID=52) you using Intimidation, it gets a critical failure instead (so it can’t try to Coerce you again for 1 week).
