<Feat title={"Stonemason's Eye"} traits={[['Dwarf']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4392"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 44

  You understand the intricacies of stonework. You become trained in [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4). If you're already trained in Crafting, you instead gain the [Specialty Crafting](/Feats.aspx?ID=844) skill feat for stonemasonry.

You also gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception checks to notice unusual stonework. This bonus applies to checks to discover mechanical traps made of stone or hidden within stone.

If you aren't using the [Seek](/Actions.aspx?ID=84) action or searching, the GM automatically rolls a secret check for you to notice unusual stonework anyway. This check doesn't gain your normal circumstance bonus.
