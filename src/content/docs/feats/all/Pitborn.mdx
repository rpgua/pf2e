<Feat title={"Pitborn"} traits={[['Lineage'], ['Nephilim']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4547"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 79

  Your blood bears the mark of a [demon](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=28), a living embodiment of sin from the fetid depths of the [Outer Rifts](/Planes.aspx?ID=13). Demonic power pulses through your veins and manifests in a different way for each pitborn, whether you have webbed fingers and thrive in the water, large hands capable of wrestling larger foes, or some other manifestation. You are trained in [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3). If you were already trained in Athletics (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

You also gain any one common 1st-level skill feat with a prerequisite of trained in Athletics, as reflects the manifestation of your fiendish blood.
