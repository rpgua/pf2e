<Feat title={"Versatile Performance"} traits={[['Bard']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4578"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 101

 **Prerequisite:** [polymath muse](/Muses.aspx?ID=3)

 You can rely on the grandeur of your performances rather than ordinary social skills. You can use [Performance](/Skills.aspx?ID=45) instead of [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=39) to [Make an Impression](/Actions.aspx?ID=2392) and instead of [Intimidation](/Skills.aspx?ID=40) to [Demoralize](/Actions.aspx?ID=2395). You can also use an acting Performance instead of [Deception](/Skills.aspx?ID=38) to [Impersonate](/Actions.aspx?ID=2388). In addition, you can use your proficiency rank in Performance to meet the prerequisites of skill feats that require a particular proficiency rank in Deception, Diplomacy, or Intimidation.

 </Feat>
