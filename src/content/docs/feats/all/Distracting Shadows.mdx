  You have learned to remain hidden by using larger folk as a distraction to avoid drawing attention to yourself. You can use creatures that are at least one [size](/Rules.aspx?ID=445) larger than you (usually Medium or larger) as cover for the [Hide](/Actions.aspx?ID=62) and [Sneak](/Actions.aspx?ID=63) actions, though you still can't use such creatures as cover for other uses, such as the [Take Cover](/Actions.aspx?ID=90) action.
