<Feat title={"Rough Rider"} traits={[['Goblin']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4445"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 56

  You are especially good at riding traditional goblin mounts. You gain the [Ride](/Feats.aspx?ID=835) feat, even if you don't meet the prerequisites. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [Nature](/Domains.aspx?ID=22) checks to use [Command an Animal](/Actions.aspx?ID=58) on a [goblin dog](/Monsters.aspx?ID=236) or [wolf](/Monsters.aspx?ID=415) mount.

If you gain a [wolf](/AnimalCompanions.aspx?ID=8) as an animal companion, it gains the [mount](/Rules.aspx?ID=150) special ability (and if you gain an ability that grants you an animal companion with the mount special ability, you can choose a wolf).
