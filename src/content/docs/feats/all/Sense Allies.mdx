<Feat title={"Sense Allies"} traits={[['Human']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4483"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 64

  Like many humans raised in a close-knit community, you have always been strongly attuned to the presence of others. Willing allies that you are aware of within 60 feet that would otherwise be [undetected](/Conditions.aspx?ID=39) by you are instead [hidden](/Conditions.aspx?ID=22) from you. The flat check for you to target willing allies within 60 feet that are hidden from you is 5 instead of 11.
