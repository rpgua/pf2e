<Feat title={"Monstrous Peacemaker"} traits={[['Dromaar']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4571"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 83

  Your dual human and orc nature has given you a unique perspective, allowing you to bridge the gap between humans and the many intelligent creatures in the world that humans consider monsters. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) checks against non-humanoid intelligent creatures and against humanoids that are marginalized in human society (at the GM’s discretion, but typically at least including [giants](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=53), [goblins](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=55), [kobolds](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=65), and [orcs](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=80)). You also gain this bonus on [Perception](/Rules.aspx?ID=405) checks to [Sense the Motives](/Actions.aspx?ID=85) of such creatures.
