<Feat title={"Dwarven Weapon Familiarity"} traits={[['Dwarf']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4389"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 44

  Your kin have instilled in you an affinity for hard-hitting weapons, and you prefer these to more elegant arms. You gain access to all uncommon weapons with the [dwarf](/Traits.aspx?ID=54) trait. You have familiarity with weapons with the dwarf trait plus the [battle axe](/Weapons.aspx?ID=16), [pick](/Weapons.aspx?ID=34), and [warhammer](/Weapons.aspx?ID=47)—for the purposes of proficiency, you treat any of these that are martial weapons as simple weapons and any that are advanced weapons as martial weapons.

At 5th level, whenever you get a critical hit with one of these weapons, you get its critical specialization effect.
