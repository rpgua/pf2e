<Feat title={"Blessed Blood"} traits={[['Nephilim']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4553"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 80

  Your spilled blood is sanctified, with effects similar to those of [holy water](/Equipment.aspx?ID=245). Whenever a [fiend](/Traits.aspx?ID=70), [undead](/Traits.aspx?ID=160), or creature with a weakness to holy drinks your blood or deals piercing or slashing damage to you with jaws, fangs, or a similar attack, that creature takes 1d6 spirit damage with the [holy](/Traits.aspx?ID=517) trait. You gain a +4 circumstance bonus to [Crafting](/Skills.aspx?ID=4) checks to [Craft](/Actions.aspx?ID=43) holy water using your blood as one of the ingredients.
