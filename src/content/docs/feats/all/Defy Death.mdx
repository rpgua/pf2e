<Feat title={"Defy Death"} traits={[['Orc']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4522"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 72

 **Prerequisite:** [Orc Ferocity](/Feats.aspx?ID=4514)

 You’re exceptionally difficult to kill. The DC of your [recovery checks](/Rules.aspx?ID=375) is equal to 9 + your [dying](/Conditions.aspx?ID=11) value, or 8 + your dying value if you have the [Toughness](/Feats.aspx?ID=855) general feat. In addition, whenever someone returns you to life using magic that would normally leave you debilitated for a week (such as [raise dead](/Spells.aspx?ID=243) or the [resurrect](/Rituals.aspx?ID=20) ritual), you don’t suffer that condition.

 </Feat>
