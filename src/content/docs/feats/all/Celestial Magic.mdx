<Feat title={"Celestial Magic"} traits={[['Nephilim']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={9} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4558"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 80

 **Prerequisite:** [Angelkin](/Feats.aspx?ID=4542), [Lawbringer](/Feats.aspx?ID=4545), [Musetouched](/Feats.aspx?ID=4546), or another lineage feat associated with celestials

 You possess celestial magic. Choose two of the following spells: [_clear mind_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1469), [_everlight_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1518), [_humanoid form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1560), [_revealing light_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1653), [_share life_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1669), or [_sure footing_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1708). You can use each of the chosen spells once per day as 2nd-rank divine [innate spells](/Rules.aspx?ID=279). [Angelkin](/Feats.aspx?ID=1348) typically take _clear mind_ and _humanoid form_, [lawbringers](/Feats.aspx?ID=1352) typically have _everlight_ and _share life_, and [musetouched](/Feats.aspx?ID=4546) typically have _revealing light_ and _sure footing_.

 </Feat>
