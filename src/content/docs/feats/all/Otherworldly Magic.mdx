  Your elven magic manifests as a simple arcane spell, even if you aren't formally trained in magic. Choose one cantrip from the [arcane](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=1) spell list. You can cast this cantrip as an arcane innate spell at will. A cantrip is heightened to a spell rank equal to half your level rounded up.
