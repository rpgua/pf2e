 **Source:** Player Core pg. 45

  Your hardiness lets you withstand more punishment than most. Increase your maximum Hit Points by your level. When you have the [dying](/Conditions.aspx?ID=11) condition, the DC of your recovery checks is equal to 9 + your dying value (instead of 10 + your dying value).

If you also have the [Toughness](/Feats.aspx?ID=855) feat, the Hit Points gained from it and this feat are cumulative, and the DC of your recovery checks is equal to 6 + your dying value.
