<Feat title={"Gnome Weapon Familiarity"} traits={[['Gnome']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4427"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 52

  You favor unusual weapons tied to your people, such as blades with curved and peculiar shapes. You gain access to [kukris](/Weapons.aspx?ID=56) and all uncommon weapons with the [gnome](/Traits.aspx?ID=80) trait. You have familiarity with weapons with the gnome trait plus the [glaive](/Weapons.aspx?ID=20) and kukri—for the purposes of proficiency, you treat any of these that are martial weapons as simple weapons and any that are advanced weapons as martial weapons.

At 5th level, whenever you get a critical hit with one of these weapons, you get its critical specialization effect.
