 **Source:** Player Core pg. 80

  You know the secrets of [the Multiverse](/Rules.aspx?ID=1153). You gain the trained proficiency rank in either [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) or [Intimidation](/Skills.aspx?ID=7), and [Religion](/Skills.aspx?ID=13). If you would automatically become trained in one of those skills (from your background or class, for example), you instead become trained in a skill of your choice.

You also gain the [Additional Lore](/Feats.aspx?ID=750) general feat for a [Lore](/Skills.aspx?ID=8) subcategory of a plane to which you trace your lineage.
