  Your blood sings with the liberating power of the [azatas](/MonsterFamilies.aspx?ID=10), living embodiments of freedom from the wild realm of [Elysium](/Planes.aspx?ID=16). You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [Escape](/Actions.aspx?ID=79). When you roll a critical failure on a check to Escape, you get a failure instead, and when you roll a success, you get a critical success instead.
