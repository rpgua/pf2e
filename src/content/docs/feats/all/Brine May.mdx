<Feat title={"Brine May"} traits={[['Changeling'], ['Lineage']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4530"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 77

  Your mother was a [sea hag](/Monsters.aspx?ID=254), granting you one sea-green or blue eye. You find comfort both in the sea and on the shore. Whenever you roll a success on an [Athletics](/Skills.aspx?ID=3) check to [Swim](/Actions.aspx?ID=39), you get a critical success instead. If you end your turn in water without having succeeded at a Swim action that round, you don’t sink (but you might still be moved by the current).
