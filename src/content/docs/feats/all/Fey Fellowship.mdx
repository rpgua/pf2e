<Feat title={"Fey Fellowship"} traits={[['Gnome']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4424"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 52

  Your enhanced fey connection (either through your blood or via prolonged contact with their kind) affords you a warmer reception from creatures of the First World as well as tools to foil their tricks or withstand their magic. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to both Perception checks and saving throws against fey.

In addition, whenever you meet a fey creature in a social situation, you can immediately attempt a [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) check to [Make an Impression](/Actions.aspx?ID=50) on that creature rather than needing to converse for 1 minute. You take a –5 penalty to the check. If you fail, you can engage in 1 minute of conversation and attempt a new check at the end of that time rather than accepting the failure or critical failure result.
