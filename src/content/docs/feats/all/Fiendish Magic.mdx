<Feat title={"Fiendish Magic"} traits={[['Nephilim']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={9} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4561"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 81

 **Prerequisite:** [Grimspawn](/Feats.aspx?ID=4543), [Pitborn](/Feats.aspx?ID=4547), or [Hellspawn](/Feats.aspx?ID=4544)

 You possess fiendish magic. Choose two of the following spells: [_disguise magic_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1491), [_false vitality_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1523), [_invisibility_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1577), [_see the unseen_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1663), [_shatter_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1670), or [_paranoia_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1623). You can use each of the chosen spells once per day as 2nd-rank divine [innate spells](/Rules.aspx?ID=279). [Grimspawn](/Feats.aspx?ID=1380) typically take _false vitality_ and _see the unseen_, [pitborn](/Feats.aspx?ID=1383) typically take _paranoia_ and _shatter_, and [hellspawn](/Feats.aspx?ID=1381) typically take _invisibility_ and _disguise_.

 </Feat>
