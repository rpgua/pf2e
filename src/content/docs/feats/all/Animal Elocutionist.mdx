  You hear animal sounds as conversations instead of unintelligent noise, and you can respond in turn. You can ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) skill with animals. In most cases, wild animals will give you time to make your case. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [Make an Impression](/Actions.aspx?ID=50) on animals.
