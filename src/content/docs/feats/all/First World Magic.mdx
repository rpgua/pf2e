 **Source:** Player Core pg. 52

  Your connection to the First World grants you a primal innate spell, much like those of the fey. Choose one cantrip from the [primal](/SpellLists.aspx?Tradition=4) spell list. You can cast this spell as a primal innate spell at will. A cantrip is heightened to a spell rank equal to half your level rounded up.
