 **Source:** Player Core pg. 61

  You are easily able to ward off attempts to play on your fears and emotions. When you roll a success on a saving throw against an [emotion](/Traits.aspx?ID=60) effect, you get a critical success instead. If your heritage is [gutsy halfling](/Heritages.aspx?ID=21), when you roll a critical failure on a saving throw against an emotion effect, you get a failure instead.
