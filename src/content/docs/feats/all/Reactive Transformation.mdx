<Feat title={"Reactive Transformation"} traits={[['Druid']]} action={1} pfs={"Standard"} level={14} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4754"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 134

 **Prerequisite:** [Untamed Form](/Feats.aspx?ID=4713); [Dragon Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=4749), [Elemental Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=4741), [Plant Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=4744), or [Soaring Shape](/Feats.aspx?ID=4739)

**Trigger:** varies

 You transform reflexively when in danger. You cast [_untamed form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1861) to transform into one shape granted by one of the prerequisite feats you have, depending on the trigger. Your shape’s resistances and weaknesses apply against the triggering damage.
<ul><li>**Trigger** You fall 10 feet or more; Effect Choose a shape from [_aerial form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1437).</li><li>**Trigger** You take [acid](/Traits.aspx?ID=3), [cold](/Traits.aspx?ID=27), [electricity](/Traits.aspx?ID=56), [fire](/Traits.aspx?ID=72), or [poison](/Traits.aspx?ID=126) damage; Effect Choose a shape from [_dragon form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1502) that resists the triggering damage.</li><li>**Trigger** You take fire damage; Effect Choose a fire elemental shape from [_elemental form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1510).</li><li>**Trigger** You take poison damage; Effect Choose a shape from [_plant form_](/Spells.aspx?ID=1637).</li></ul>
