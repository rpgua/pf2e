<Feat title={"Unusual Composition"} traits={[['Bard'], ['Concentrate'], ['Manipulate'], ['Spellshape']]} action={2} pfs={"Standard"} level={10} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4616"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 105

 **Prerequisite:** [polymath muse](/Muses.aspx?ID=3)

 You can translate the emotion and power of a composition to other mediums. If your next action is to cast a [composition](/Traits.aspx?ID=559) spell that is [auditory](/Traits.aspx?ID=541) or [visual](/Traits.aspx?ID=727), you can have it affect creatures who can witness it with any sense, not just those the traits indicate, even including [special senses](/Rules.aspx?ID=2409) like [tremorsense](/Rules.aspx?ID=2413). You can choose to exclude any senses you choose.
