<Feat title={"Illusion Sense"} traits={[['Gnome']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={1} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4428"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 52

  Your ancestors spent their days cloaked and cradled in illusions, and as a result, sensing illusion magic is second nature to you. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to both Perception checks and Will saves against illusions.

When you come within 10 feet of an illusion that can be disbelieved, the GM rolls a secret check for you to disbelieve it, even if you didn't spend an action to [Interact](/Actions.aspx?ID=80) with the illusion.
