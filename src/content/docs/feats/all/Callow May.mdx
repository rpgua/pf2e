  You are among the most common of changelings—those born to sweet hags—and a vibrant green eye signifies that parentage. Your mother’s manipulative nature and versatile voice gives you an edge when deceiving others. You gain the [Charming Liar](/Feats.aspx?ID=766) skill feat. If you roll [Deception](/Skills.aspx?ID=5) for initiative, foes in that combat encounter that haven’t acted yet are [off-guard](/Conditions.aspx?ID=58) against you.
