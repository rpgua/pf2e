<Feat title={"Speak with Kindred"} traits={[['Leshy']]} pfs={"Standard"} level={5} url={"https://2e.aonprd.com/Feats.aspx?ID=4503"}>
 **Source:** Player Core pg. 69

  You have a connection with creatures that share your physiology. You can ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the [Diplomacy](/Skills.aspx?ID=6) skill with plants and fungi. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Diplomacy checks with plants or fungi of your kind. Generally, [fungus leshies](/Heritages.aspx?ID=45) get this bonus with mushrooms and fungi; [gourd leshies](/Heritages.aspx?ID=46) with gourds, melons, and similar fruiting plants; [leaf leshies](/Heritages.aspx?ID=47) with deciduous trees; and [vine leshies](/Heritages.aspx?ID=48) with vines and climbing plants.
